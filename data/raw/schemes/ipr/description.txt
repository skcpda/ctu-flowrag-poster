The name of this welfare scheme is Intellectual Property Right Scheme Under MSME Innovative Scheme. It is also known as IPR. Launched by the Ministry of Micro, Small &amp;amp; Medium Enterprises , Intellectual Property Right Scheme offers legal and intellectual property filing support including patents, trademarks, copyrights, designs, geographical indications (GI) etc. The programme also provides IP advisory, consultation, Patentability Searches, Technology Gap Analyses and IP commercialisation through the establishment of Intellectual Property Facilitation Centres (IPFCs) across the country.
Objective:
The objective of the scheme is to improve the IP culture in India with the following interventions:
• To enhance the awareness of Intellectual Property Rights (IPRs) amongst the MSMEs and to encourage creative intellectual endeavour in Indian economy;
• To take suitable measures for the protection of ideas, technological innovation and knowledge-driven business strategies developed by the MSMEs for their commercialization and effective utilization of IPR tools.
Activities:
• Establishment of Intellectual Property Facilitation Centres (IPFCs).
• Reimbursement for registration of Patent, Trademark, Geographical Indications (G.I.), Design through IPFCs. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Business Entity. Additionally, the nodal ministry is Ministry Of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises.

The scheme provides the following benefits: A Grant of up to ₹ 1,00,00,000/would be provided to an IPFC in milestonebased (three or more) instalments.
Reimbursement for registration of Patent, Trademark, Geographical Indications (G.I.), and Design: The maximum financial assistance to the eligible applicants under the IPR component is as follows:
S. No.ItemMaximum Financial AssistanceForeign Patent₹ 5,000,00/Domestic Patent₹ 1,000,00/GI Registration₹ 2,000,00/Design Registration₹ 15,000/

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary unit(s) must typically be a registered micro, small or medium enterprises.
Should have a valid Udyog Aadhaar Memorandum (UAM) or Udyam Registration certificate. The application process is as follows: Registration Process:
Step 01: After opening the website https://innovative.msme.gov.in/ , click on Intellectual Property Rights (IPR).
Step 02: On the next window ,scroll down click on For registration as IPFC.
Step 03: Fill all the required details and click on Submit.
Step 04: Click on Validate.
