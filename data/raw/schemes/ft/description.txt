The name of this welfare scheme is Farmers Training. It is also known as FT. The “Farmers Training” scheme, by the Agriculture-Farmers Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu, offers training to farmers and agricultural laborers to enhance their skills and knowledge. Each training session supports up to 50 participants, with a grant of ₹5,000/- provided per session to cover the costs.
&lt;br&gt; The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Agriculture Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Financial assistance: ₹5,000/per training session of 50 Farmers/Agricultural labourers.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Farmer.
  The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
  The beneficiary should have interest in joining the training to enhance agricultural skills. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Collect the application form from the nearest Agricultural Office.
Step 2: Fill out the application form with the required details and attach mandatory documents (selfattested, if required). 
Step 3: Submit the form to the concerned office:
&gt; Village Level: Submit the application to the Assistant Agricultural Officer.

&gt; Block Level: Submit to the Agricultural Officer or Deputy Agricultural Officer.

&gt; District Level: Submit to the Joint Director of Agriculture. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://www.tn.gov.in/scheme/data_view/6847 Tags associated with the scheme include: Agriculture, Farmer, Training.
