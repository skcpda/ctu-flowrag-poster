The name of this welfare scheme is ICAR - Post Matric Scholarship For Scheduled Caste / Scheduled Tribes Candidates. It is also known as ICAR-PMS-SC/STC. The scheme &quot;Post-Matric Scholarship to Scheduled Castes / Scheduled Tribes Candidates&quot; was introduced by the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR), Department of Agricultural Research and Education (DARE), to enable the students belonging to the weaker sections of society to complete undergraduate courses. The scholarship is provided for studying at the Bachelor&#39;s Degree program in various branches of Agriculture viz. B.Sc.(Agri.), B.V.Sc., B.Sc.(Agril. Engg.), B.Sc.(Home Science), B.F.Sc., B.Sc.(Forestry) etc. The value of the scholarship will be ₹ 1,000/- per month along with a contingent grant of ₹ 750/- per annum for the payment of tuition fees, purchase of books, cost of uniforms, etc. The duration of the scholarship will cover the whole of the normal period of the particular degree program to which the candidate is admitted. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. The implementing agency is Indian Council Of Agricultural Research. Additionally, the nodal ministry is Ministry Of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare and the nodal department is Department of Agriculture Research and Education.

It falls under the categories: Education & Learning.

Eligibility criteria include: This scholarship is exclusively meant for students belonging to Scheduled Castes / Scheduled Tribes communities as defined by the Government of India. Only those students who have been admitted to Agricultural Universities in any of the undergraduate degrees programmes of Agriculture &amp; Allied Sciences will be eligible for this scholarship For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://education.icar.gov.in/Files/Guidelines/Post-matric-scholarship-guidelines.pdf Nodal Officers: https://education.icar.gov.in/nodal_officer.aspx Proforma For Submission Of Demand: https://education.icar.gov.in/pdf/format-PMS-2017.pdf Revised Rate Of Scholarship: https://education.icar.gov.in/files/Circular/office_order_hrd.pdf Tags associated with the scheme include: Agriculture, Post Matric, Scheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribe, Scholarship.
