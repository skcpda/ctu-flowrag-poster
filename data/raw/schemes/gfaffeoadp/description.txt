The name of this welfare scheme is Grant Of Financial Assistance For The Funeral Expenses Of Old Age/ Destitute Pensioner. It is also known as Gfaffeoadp. The Government of Puducherry introduced the Grant Of Financial Assistance For The Funeral Expenses Of Old Age/Destitute Pensioner Scheme to extend a helping hand by way of monthly financial assistance of ₹.500/- to Old Age/Destitute Pensioners who are suffering from T.B./Cancer to meet out the medical expenses. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Puducherry. Additionally, the nodal department is Women and Child Development Department, Puducherry.

The scheme provides the following benefits: ₹ Rs.500/pm in addition to the old age pension amount.

Eligibility criteria include: Old Age and Destitute pensioners suffering from T.B./Cancer. The application process is as follows: > Whom to Apply


Puducherry: The Deputy Director (Social Defence), Old Age Pension Section, Department of Women and Child Development, Puducherry.Karaikal: The Child Development Project Officer, Karaikal.Mahe/Yanam: The Welfare Officer, Mahe/ Yanam.
> Processing Time
