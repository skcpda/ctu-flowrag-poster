The name of this welfare scheme is Mukhyamantri - Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana. It is also known as MMLBY. The &quot;Mukhyamantri - Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana&quot; scheme was launched by the Women and Child Development Department, Government of Maharashtra. The scheme aims to give economic freedom to women in the state age group of 21 to 65 years, improving their health and nutrition and strengthening their decisive role in the family. The women will get a financial benefit of ₹1,500/- through direct benefit transfer (DBT). The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Maharashtra. Additionally, the nodal department is Women and Child Development Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Financial Assistance of ₹1,500/per month.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a female.
The applicant should be a resident of Maharashtra state.
The applicant&#39;s age should be between 2165 years.
The applicant should have their bank account with an Aadhaar link.
The annual income of the applicant&#39;s family should not exceed ₹2,50,000/. 
Outsourced employees, voluntary workers, and contract workers with income up to ₹2,50,000/are eligible.
The applicant should be any one of the following:



  Married Woman 
  Widowed 
  Divorced Woman
  Abandoned and Destitute Women 
  One Unmarried Woman in the family The application process is as follows: Registration Process:
Step 01: The interested applicant visit the official website. 
Step 02: Select &quot;Applicant Login&quot; and click on &quot;Create Account.&quot;
Step 03: Complete the registration form with the following details: Full Name as per Aadhaar, Mobile No, Password, Confirm Password, District, Taluka, Village, Municipal Corporation / Council, Authorized Person &amp; Accept Terms and Conditions. 
Step 04: Enter the Captcha code and click &quot;Signup.&quot; You will receive an OTP.
Step 05: Enter the OTP and the Captcha code again.
Step 06: Click &quot;Verify the OTP&quot; to receive a message confirming that your login was successful.


Application Process:
Step 01: Log in to the portal using your Mobile number, Password, and Captcha to log in.
Step 02: Click on &quot;Application of Mukhyamantri Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana&quot; and enter your Aadhaar Number and Captcha.
Step 03: Click on &quot;Valid Aadhaar&quot; and complete the registration form with the applicant&#39;s name, bank details, and permanent address.
Step 04: Upload relevant documents such as an Aadhaar card, Domicile certificate, Ration card, etc.
Step 05: Click &quot;Submit&quot; to receive your application ID via SMS.


Check the Status:
Step 01: Log in to the portal using your Mobile number, Password, and Captcha to log in.
Step 02: Click on &quot;Applications Made Earlier&quot; to view the status of your applications.
