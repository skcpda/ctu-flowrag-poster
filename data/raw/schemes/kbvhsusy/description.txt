The name of this welfare scheme is Kachara Binane Vaale Hetu Suraksha Upakaran Sahaayata Yojana. It is also known as KBVHSUSY. Labor Department of Chhattisgarh State started this scheme &amp;quot;Kachara Beenane Vaale Hetu Suraksha Upakaran Sahaayata Yojana&amp;quot; for unorganized worker on 19 June 2017. The main objective of the scheme to empower unorganized worker by providing safety equipment. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Gloves, Shoes and Rain Coat (OR)
A cash assistance in the amount determined by the CSID.

Eligibility criteria include: Applicant must be a registered waste picker in Chhattisgarh State.
Applicant must not be receiving assistance from any other parallel scheme of the state government.
Applicant must be between 18 and 60 years old. The application process is as follows: Unregistered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Asangathit Shramik Panjikaran&quot;
   What do you want to do :  &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Check the eligibility criteria for registration in Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board and click next. 
Fill out the online &quot;Registration form&quot;.
Submit.



Already Registered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Yojana&quot;
   What do you want to do :  &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Select your district name and provide old/new registration number and click next.
Select the scheme name. 
Fill out the online with required details. 
Submit.
