The name of this welfare scheme is Post Metric Scholarship For OBC Students. It is also known as PMS. Madhya Pradesh State government started a scholarship for student who belong to the OBC category. Now the students of the state will not be deprived of education due to financial constraints, because the Madhya Pradesh government will provide scholarships to the students. Through the scholarship, the state&amp;#39;s literacy rate will also increase and also will encourage the student to higher education. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Madhya Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is School Education Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: The benefit of the scholarship depends on the annual family income of the students, institution, course, course subscriber, fee capping, etc.
All students whose annual family income is up to Rs 3,00,000, those students will be eligible for a 100% scholarship.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant must be a permanent resident of Madhya Pradesh.
   Annual family income is up to Rs 3,00,000/Have passed Class Belong to the OBC category. The application process is as follows: Applicants have to visit the official website. Applicants need to register himself/herself.
Fill out the required details.
Now login with credentials and fill out the application form.
