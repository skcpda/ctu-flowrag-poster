The name of this welfare scheme is Capacity Building For Service Providers - Entrepreneurship Programme. It is also known as CBSP-EP. The scheme &quot;Entrepreneurship Programme&quot; is a Suo-Moto Sub-Scheme of the Ministry of Tourism under the Umbrella Scheme of &quot;Capacity Building for Service Providers (CBSP)&quot;. In this scheme, financial assistance is provided to the institutes (given below) to conduct entrepreneurship development programmes - 
1.  Government-Sponsored Tourism &amp; Hospitality Institutes
2.  Kerala Institute of Tourism &amp; Travel Studies (KITTS)
3.  India Tourism Development Corporation (ITDC)


The target group are the citizens who have passed Class 8th and who are above the age of 18 years. The programme has 150 Hours of courses in the following five trades - (i) Cook - Tandoor (ii) Barman (iii) Baker (iv) Homestay (Multi-Skilled Caretaker) and (v) Halwai - Indian Sweets. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Institution. Additionally, the nodal ministry is Ministry Of Tourism.

It falls under the categories: Skills & Employment.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should have passed Class 8th.
The applicant should be above the age of 18 years.
