The name of this welfare scheme is Sudama Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme (Class 9th to 10th). It is also known as SPMSSC910. The scheme &amp;quot;Sudama Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme&amp;quot; was launched by the School Education Department, Government of Madhya Pradesh, to motivate students from lower economic backgrounds and support them economically. Under this scheme, scholarships are provided to students studying in Class 9 and Class 10 in government schools, whose annual family income is less than ₹1,00,000/- and who belong to the Below Poverty Line (BPL) category. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Madhya Pradesh. The implementing agency is School Education Department, Government Of Madhya Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is School Education Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Under this scheme, the girl student will get a scholarship of ₹400/and boy student will get a scholarship of ₹300/per year.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a resident of Madhya Pradesh.
   The applicant should be studying in Class 9 or Class The student should be enrolled in a government school.
   The applicant should belong to the Below Poverty Line (BPL) category.
   The applicant&amp;#39;s family income from all sources should be less than ₹1,00,000/per year.
   The applicant should have passed the previous year&amp;#39;s class. The application process is as follows: To avail of the benefits under this scheme, eligible students can contact the Principal of their respective government schools.
