The name of this welfare scheme is Mukhya Mantri Grihini Suvidha Yojana. It is also known as MMGSY. The Yojana aims to free women of Himachal Pradesh from the indoor pollution by providing free LPG connections to families with no gas connection.
The objective of launching the Mukhya Mantri Grihini Suvidha Yojna was to provide free gas connections to those families who were not covered under the Centre&#39;s Ujjwala scheme. The state government started this scheme on the lines of PMUY. The Mukhya Mantri Grihini Suvidha Yojana of the State Government have proved to be a boon for the women of Himachal.
As a result of Mukhya Mantri Grihini Suvidha Yojna, the dependence on wood for fuel has reduced. Besides this, the adverse effect of smoke on the health of women has also been relieved and improved the standard of living of women. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual, Family. It is specifically implemented in Himachal Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is Information and Public Relations Department, Himachal Pradesh.

It falls under the categories: Utility & Sanitation, Women and Child.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a resident of Himachal Pradesh.
The applicant should belong to the Below Poverty Line (BPL) category.
The applicant should not have an LPG connection in their name or in the name of any member of their household.
The applicant should not be a recipient of any other LPG connection under any other government scheme Tags associated with the scheme include: Gas Connection, Grihini Suvidha Yojana, Information And Public Relations, LPG Cylinder, MMGSY.
