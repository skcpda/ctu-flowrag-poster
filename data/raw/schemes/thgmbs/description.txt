The name of this welfare scheme is Theka Shramik, Hamaal Shramik Evan Gharelu Mahila Kamgar Gambhir Bimaari Chikitsa Sahayata Yojana. It is also known as THGMBS. Labor Department of Chhattisgarh State started this scheme &amp;amp;quot;Theka Shramik, Hamaal Shramik Evan Gharelu Mahila Kamgar Gambhir Bimaari Chikitsa Sahayata Yojana&amp;amp;quot; for unorganized worker on 05 June 2018. The main objective of the scheme to provides financial assistance for serious illness. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Medical aid of â‚¹ 50,000/will be provided for the treatment of registered contract workers, Hamal workers and female domestic workers who are suffering from serious illness.
Serious diseases under the scheme will include kidney, cancer, sickle cell anemia, heart disease, AIDS and paralysis.

Eligibility criteria include: Applicant must be registered with the State Social Security Board at least 90 days in advance.
Applicant must be in the age group of 18 to 60 years will be eligible for this scheme.
Applicant can be only received benefits at once. 
If applicant received from any other parallel scheme of the State Government, then he/she is not eligible for this scheme. The application process is as follows: Unregistered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Asangathit Shramik Panjikaran&quot;
   What do you want to do : &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Check the eligibility criteria for registration in Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board and click next. 
Fill out the online &quot;Registration form&quot;.
Submit.



Already Registered Applicant :
