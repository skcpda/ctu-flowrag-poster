The name of this welfare scheme is West Bengal Migrant Workers’ Welfare Scheme: Cremation. It is also known as WBMWWSCA. &amp;quot;West Bengal Migrant Workers’ Welfare Scheme: Cremation&amp;quot; is a social welfare scheme implemented by the West Bengal Migrant Workers’ Welfare Board, Labour Department, Government of West Bengal on 25th April, 2023. Under this scheme, if a registered Migrant Worker dies at his / her work place or the place he /she had to stay for the purpose of his / her job, an assistance amounting to ₹ 3,000/- will be provided to the nominee of the worker for cremation at the place, the worker was employed. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in West Bengal. The implementing agency is West Bengal Migrant Workers’ Welfare Board. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: ₹ 3,000/to the nominee of the registered Migrant Worker.

Eligibility criteria include: > For registration as a Migrant Worker:


  The applicant must be a resident of West Bengal.
  The applicant’s age should be between 1860 years.
  The applicant must be ordinarily a worker who is employed/ likely to be employed in any State/Union Territories other than the state of West Bengal/ Outside India.



> For the application of Welfare Scheme:


  The beneficiary should be a nominee i.e., a person nominated by the registered Migrant Worker from his/ her family.
