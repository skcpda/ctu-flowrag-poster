The name of this welfare scheme is Widow Re-marriage Scheme. It is also known as WRMS. The Widow Re-Marriage Scheme of Himachal Pradesh is a government scheme aimed at providing financial assistance to widows who have remarried. The primary objective of this scheme is to help in the rehabilitation of widows after remarriage. 


Widow Re-Marriage Scheme launched by the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of Himachal Pradesh. The main purpose of the scheme is to help in the rehabilitation of widows, by encouraging men to enter into wedlock with widows. This scheme provides financial assistance to such widows. Under this scheme financial assistance of Rs. 25, 000 is provided out of which cash grant of Rs. 10,000 will be given to widow at the time of marriage and Rs. 15,000 will be kept in the form of Fixed Deposit jointly at least for five years. The initiative has helped many destitute widows of the state. All widow permanent residents of Himachal can apply to the scheme. More details and application form can be obtained at nearest District welfare office, the Government of Himachal Pradesh. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Himachal Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is Department of Social Justice and Empowerment.

It falls under the categories: Social welfare & Empowerment, Women and Child.

Eligibility criteria include: The widow should have remarried.
The applicant should be a resident of Himachal Pradesh.
Men not less than 21 years of age
Women not less than 18 years of age.
