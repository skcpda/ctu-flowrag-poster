The name of this welfare scheme is Grant of Marriage Allowance to the Widow's Daughter. It is also known as GMAWD. The scheme “Grant of Marriage Allowance to the Widow&#39;s Daughter (GMAWD)” is a financial assistance scheme by the Department of Women and Child Development, Government of Puducherry. This scheme aims to provide financial support to widows for the marriage of their daughters. Eligible widows can receive ₹25,000/- to cover marriage expenses. The scheme is implemented through Puducherry&#39;s Department of Women and Child Development. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Puducherry. Additionally, the nodal department is Women and Child Development Department, Puducherry.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Financial assistance of ₹25,000/for marriage expenses.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a widow with an annual income not exceeding ₹75,000/.
The widow should be a citizen of India and a native of the Union territory of Puducherry by birth or continuous residence of 5 years or more.
The allowance is applicable only for the first marriage of the bride.
The marriage must be performed in accordance with the law.
The allowance is granted for the marriage of one daughter only.
