The name of this welfare scheme is Mukh‍yamantri Janakal‍yaan (Sambal 2.0) Yojana (Asangathit Karmakaar Man‍da). It is also known as MJSYAKM. The Mukhyamantri Jan Kalyan (Sambal 2.0) Yojana (Asangathit Karmakar Mandal) is a scheme launched by the Madhya Pradesh government to provide social security to unorganized workers in the state. This scheme covers unorganized workers from specific age group who are not covered under any other social security scheme and benefits will be provided directly into the beneficiary&#39;s account through the DBT system. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Madhya Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Funeral Grant Amount : ₹5,000/Exgratia
On accidental death : ₹4,00,000/General Death Benefit : ₹2,00,000/Permanent Disability Benefit : ₹2,00,000/Partial Permanent Disability Benefit : ₹1,00,000/

Eligibility criteria include: Applicant must be a Unorganized worker.
Applicant have a &quot;Samagra ID&quot; and &quot;Pariwar ID&quot;.
Applicant should not have other social security cover like Provident Fund, Employees&#39; State Insurance Scheme, Gratuity etc
Applicant or his wife/husband (as the case may be) should not follow below categories

   Should not have more than 01 hectare of agricultural land,
   Should not be in government service
   Should not an income tax payer.
   Should not have other social security cover like Provident Fund, Employees&#39; State Insurance Scheme, Gratuity etc The application process is as follows: > Samagra ID Creation:


Visit the official website and click on the &quot;Register family&quot;.On the new page, enter the Mobile number which was linked with Aadhaar Number and authenticate via OTP.
Now provide the Aadhaar number and enter the new OTP.
On the new page, provide the basic details, complete address
Give the consent and submit.



> Apply Process:


Visit the official Sambal Portal.Under &quot;Sevaaye&quot;, click on &quot;Panchikaran Hetu Aavedan Kren&quot;.Enter &quot;Samagra ID&quot; and &quot;Pariwar ID&quot;.
Fill out the online application form with required details.
Give the consent and submit.
