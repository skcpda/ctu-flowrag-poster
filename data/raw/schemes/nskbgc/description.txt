The name of this welfare scheme is Nirman Shramikon Ke Bacchon Hetu Nishulk Ganvesh Evam Pustak Copy Hetu Sahaayata Raashi Yojana. It is also known as NSKBGC. Labor Department of Chhattisgarh State started this scheme &amp;amp;quot;Nirman Shramikon Ke Bacchon Hetu Nishulk Ganvesh Evam Pustak Copy Hetu Sahaayata Raashi Yojana&amp;amp;quot; for Construction Workers on 09 December 2022. The main objective of the scheme to provide financial assistance for the purpose of providing educational aids like uniforms and stationery.
&amp;lt;br&amp;gt; The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: The proposed assistance amount for school uniform and book copy will be payable in lump sum as mentioned below:
ClassesAmountClass 1 to 8₹ 1,000/Class 9 to 12₹ 2,000/

Eligibility criteria include: Applicant must be a registered construction worker under the Chhattisgarh Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board.
Applicant&#39;s children must be studying in classes 1 to 12 in any recognized school in Chhattisgarh.
