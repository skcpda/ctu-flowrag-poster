The name of this welfare scheme is Vahli Dikri Yojana. It is also known as VDY. Objectives 

   1. To promote the education and empowerment of girl children in the state of Gujarat.
   1. To reduce the financial burden on families with girl children.
   1. To encourage families to celebrate the birth of girl children and reduce the prevalence of gender discrimination and female infanticide.
   1. To improve the overall welfare of girl children in the state.

&lt;br&gt;

Target Beneficiary:
The target beneficiaries of the Vahli Dikri Yojana scheme are families with one or two girl children in the state of Gujarat.
&lt;br&gt;

&lt;br&gt; The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Family. It is specifically implemented in Gujarat. Additionally, the nodal department is Women and Child Development Department.

It falls under the categories: Education & Learning, Social welfare & Empowerment.
