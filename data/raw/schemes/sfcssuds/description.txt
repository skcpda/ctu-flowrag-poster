The name of this welfare scheme is Scheme for Campaign, Seminar and Sports under Disability Sector. It is also known as SFCSSUDS. The &amp;quot;Scheme for Campaign, Seminar and Sports under Disability Sector&amp;quot; is a social welfare scheme, implemented by the Social Security and Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (SSEPD) Department, Government of Odisha. This scheme promotes sports, cultural activities, and various events to highlight the rights and needs of PwDs, encouraging volunteer action and ensuring effective implementation of legal provisions and welfare schemes through extending financial support to the reputed organizations or an individual person with disabilities working for welfare of Persons with Disabilities.
&lt;br&gt; The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual, NGO. It is specifically implemented in Odisha. Additionally, the nodal department is Social Security and Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: StateLevel Events: Financial assistance limited to ₹1,00,000/per event.  DistrictLevel Events: Financial assistance limited to ₹50,000/per event.

Eligibility criteria include: Institutions or an individual person with disabilities working for the welfare of Persons with Disabilities shall be eligible for financial support.
  The events should be State and District level programs. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Submit a proposal to the concerned District Social Security Officer (DSSO) at least one month before the event.
Step 2: The proposal should include details of the event and the financial assistance required.
Step 3: Ensure you receive a submission receipt for future reference (if provided).
