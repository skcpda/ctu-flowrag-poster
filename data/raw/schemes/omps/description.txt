The name of this welfare scheme is Opening And Maintenance Of Public School (Vidya Niketan) For Vimukta Jatis And Nomadic Tribes. It is also known as OMPS. The Government of Maharashtra has introduced a scheme &quot;Opening And Maintenance Of Public School (Vidya Niketan) For Vimukta Jatis And Nomadic Tribes&quot; to the voluntary organization to run the Public School (Vidya Niketan) on a grant-in-aid basis at Kamlewadi, Taluka Mukhed, District Nanded for the needy, brilliant and talented students belonging to VJNT Category. In this public school, students get an education from 5th to 12th standard on a merit basis.
The Benefits provided by the Government of Maharashtra are Financial Assistance given to the institution for maintenance assistance given on building rent. 100 percent salary grants for approved teaching and non-teaching staff. Students are provided with facilities like books, notebooks, uniforms, education stationary free lodging, and boarding facilities. Rs.1450/- p.m. per residential student is admissible. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Maharashtra. Additionally, the nodal department is Social Justice and Special Assistance Department.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a student.
The beneficiary should be a resident of Maharashtra.
The beneficiary should belong to the VJNT community.
The income of the parents should not be more than Rs. 24000/per annum.

Step 2: Click on &quot;New Applicant Registration&quot;. Register using your Name, Mobile Number, and Email ID. Create a Username and Password. Your Mobile Number and Email ID will be verified via OTP.
