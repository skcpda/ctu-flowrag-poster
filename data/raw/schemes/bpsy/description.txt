The name of this welfare scheme is Minimata Mahtari Jatan Yojana. It is also known as BPSY. The Labor Department of Chhattisgarh introduced the &quot;Minimata Mahtari Jatan Yojana&quot; on March 4, 2010, through the &quot;Building and Construction Workers Welfare Board.&quot; This initiative is designed to provide benefits to registered female construction workers during pregnancy. The scheme applies to both male and female beneficiaries who are registered and possess a beneficiary identity card at least 90 days before the scheme application date.


Note: The name of this scheme was changed from &quot;Bhagini Prasuti Sahayata Yojana&quot; to  &quot;Minimata Mahtari Jatan Yojana&quot; on May 12, 2022. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. The implementing agency is Building And Construction Workers Welfare Board. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a permanent resident of Chhattisgarh.
The applicant should be a woman worker.
The applicant should be registered  Building &amp; Other Construction Workers Welfare Board.
The benefit will only be provided for only first two children.
The applicant should be registered as a construction worker 90 days in advance.
