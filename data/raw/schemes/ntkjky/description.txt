The name of this welfare scheme is Niji Talabon Ka Jirnoddhar Ki Yojana. It is also known as NTKJKY. The &quot;Niji Talabon Ka Jirnoddhar Ki Yojana&quot; was launched by the Animal and Fisheries Resources Department, Government of Bihar. The scheme specifically allocates resources for extremely backward castes, scheduled castes, and scheduled tribes, ensuring inclusive growth and support for marginalized communities. Under the scheme, the target is to renovate 150.00 hectares of private ponds. 


> Objectives:


- Increase Water Holding Capacity: Enhance the water holding capacity of private ponds to boost fish productivity and production.

- Economic Benefits for Fish Farmers: Provide benefits to private fish farmers through the expansion/renovation of their ponds.


> Model of Pond Restoration:


- Model-1: Renovation of old private ponds (not renovated under any government scheme in the last five years).- Model-2: Renovation of private ponds constructed under government schemes (benefits payable after five years of construction). The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Bihar. Additionally, the nodal department is Animal & Fisheries Resources Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Renovation of 150 hectares of private ponds with the following categoryspecific targets:

Other category beneficiaries: 86 hectares
Extremely backward castes: 27 hectares
Scheduled castes: 30 hectares
Scheduled tribes: 7 hectares

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a fish farmer.
The applicant should be private pond owners or lessees. The application process is as follows: > Registration Process:

Step01: The applicant visits the official website and clicks the &quot;Applications are being invited for all the schemes of the financial year (202425), click here to apply&quot;. 
Step02: Select the &quot;Registration for Fisheries Schemes&quot;
Step03: Select the category: Individual and complete all basic details of the applicant for registration, including category, caste, applicant name, date of birth, address, bank details, etc.
Step04: Enter a valid 10digit mobile number and click to send OTP.
Step05: An OTP will be sent to the provided mobile number.
Step06: Enter the OTP; after verification of the OTP, you will receive a Registration Number and Password.
Step07: Click on the &quot;Login&quot; and enter the Registration Number and Password


> Application Form Filling Process:

Step01: The applicant visits the official website and clicks the &quot;Applications are being invited for all the schemes of the financial year (202425), click here to apply&quot;.
Step02: Click &quot;If your already register, login here&quot;. 
Step03: Select &quot;Scheme name&quot; under the &quot;Matsya Yojana&quot; option on the top menu.
Step04: Applicant need to provide the following details :


  Details of individual (applicant/group leader)
  Permanent address
  Bank account details
  Land/pond details


Step05: Upload the required documents.
Step06: Click on the submit button as well print the application form and submit it to the District Fisheries Office along with attachments. 


Note: Download the Affidavit form and Consent form also attached with the application.
