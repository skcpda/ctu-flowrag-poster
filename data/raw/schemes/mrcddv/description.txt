The name of this welfare scheme is Mukhyamantri Raut, Charavaaha Evan Doodh Duhane Vaale Saayakal Sahaayata Yojana. It is also known as MRCDDV. Labor Department of Chhattisgarh State started this scheme &amp;quot;Mukhyamantri Raut, Charavaaha Evan Doodh Duhane Vaale Bicycle Sahaayata Yojana&amp;quot; for unorganized worker on 10 September 2013. The main objective of the scheme to empower unorganized worker by providing financial assistance. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: > Financial Assistance of â‚¹ 3,000/per person for bicycle or the rate fixed by CSIDC for bicycle which will be lower

Eligibility criteria include: Applicant must be registered as an unorganized worker in any district of the state. 
Applicant must be between the ages of 18 and Unorganized workers who are receiving benefits from any other parallel scheme of the State Government are not eligible for the scheme. The application process is as follows: Unregistered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Asangathit Shramik Panjikaran&quot;
   What do you want to do : &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Check the eligibility criteria for registration in Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board and click next. 
Fill out the online &quot;Registration form&quot;.
Submit.



Already Registered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Yojana&quot;
   What do you want to do : &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Select your district name and provide old/new registration number and click next.
Select the scheme name. 
Fill out the online with required details. 
Submit.
