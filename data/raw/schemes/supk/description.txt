The name of this welfare scheme is Skill Upgradation Program Karnataka. It is also known as SUPK. The &quot;Skill Upgradation Program Karnataka&quot; scheme aims to upgrade the skills of artisans in leather technology, footwear/leather goods making, and designing by sending them for advanced training at renowned institutions like CFTI Chennai, CLRI, and Karnataka Institute of Leather Technology. The program enhances artisans’ skills, allowing them to contribute to the development of the leather industry through advanced training. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Karnataka. Additionally, the nodal department is Social Welfare Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Course Sponsorship: The corporation covers the entire cost of the course for selected artisans.Stipend: A stipend is provided during the course duration to support the participants

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be leather artisans.
The applicant should belongs to subcastes Arundathiyar, Chammadiya, Chamar, Chambar, Chamagar, Madar, Madig, Madiga, Mini Madig, Jambavalu, Haralayya, Machigar, Mochigar, Mochi, muchi, Telugu Mochi, kamaati mocha, Rohidas, Dhor, kakkayya, Kankayya, Samagara, Samagar, Aadhi Anddra, Aadhi Dravida, Aadhi Karnataka among schedule castes.
The applicant should have passed a Secondary School Leaving Certificate (SSLC)/PreUniversity Courses (PUC)/Degree to apply for the program.
The age of the applicant should be 18 years.
The applicant&#39;s annual family income should not exceed ₹30,000/in rural areas and ₹25,000/in urban areas.
The applicant or their family members must not have received benefits from other schemes, except training programs.
The applicant&#39;s family members should not be employed in government or semigovernment organizations. The application process is as follows: > Registration Process:

Step 01: Visit the official Seva Sindhu Portal. 
Step 02: Choose &quot;New Users Register Here&quot; and fill in your Aadhaar Number and the Captcha code.
Step 03: An OTP will be sent to your Aadhaarlinked mobile number. Enter the OTP to continue.
Step 04: Once your DigiLocker account is created, click &quot;Allow&quot; to proceed.
Step 05: Fill out your email ID, mobile number, create a password, and enter the captcha, then click &quot;Submit&quot;.
Step 06: You will receive OTPs on both your email and mobile number. Enter both OTPs and select &quot;Validate&quot;.
Step 07: Once validated, a confirmation message will indicate your registration is successful.


> Applying for a Scheme:

Step 01: Go to Seva Sindhu Portal and select &quot;Department &amp; Services&quot;.
Step 02: Select department &quot;Social Welfare Department&quot; and click on the scheme &quot;Skill Upgradation Program&quot;
Step 03: Click &quot;Apply Online&quot; and enter your registered email ID/mobile number and the OTP.
Step 04: Select the scheme and provide your personal, address, and banking details as required.
Step 05: Once submitted, you will receive an Application ID. You can use this ID to track your application status on the portal.
