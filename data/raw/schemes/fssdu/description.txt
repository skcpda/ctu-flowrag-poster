The name of this welfare scheme is Financial Support Scheme. It is also known as FSSDU. “Financial Support Scheme” implemented by the Delhi University aims to provide equity and access to quality education to the economically weak students. This scheme also provides financial support for such students which will include fee waiver of all components of fee paid by students (except examination fee &amp;amp; hostel fee). The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. The implementing agency is Delhi University. Additionally, the nodal ministry is Ministry of Education and the nodal department is Department Of Higher Education.

The scheme provides the following benefits: S. No.Family income (in ₹) per annumFee waiver (percentage)RemarksCategory 1Less than or equal to ₹ 4,00,000/Up to 100%Actual fee or up to a maximum of ₹ 10,000/Category 2Between ₹ 4,00,000/and ₹ 8,00,000/Up to 50%Actual fee or up to a maximum of ₹ 8,000/

Eligibility criteria include: Should be a bonafide student.
  The student should be studying in any UG/ PG Degree program at a Department/ Institution/Centre of University of Delhi.
  Family income of the student’s should be less than ₹ 8,00,000/. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Eligible students can fill in the online Application Form available on the website of the Dean Students’ Welfare Office.
Step 2: Students must submit a copy of their Application Form and all documents after due attestation and recommendation from the Head/ Director of the concerned Department/ Centre/ Institute at DSW office.
Step 3: The Schedule for the submission of the hard/ printed copy of the application form will be declared in the DSW website.
