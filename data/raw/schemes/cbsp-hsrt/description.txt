The name of this welfare scheme is Capacity Building For Service Providers - Hunar Se Rozgar Tak. It is also known as CBSP-HSRT. The scheme &quot;Hunar Se Rozgar Tak&quot; is a Suo-Moto Sub-Scheme of the Ministry of Tourism under the Umbrella Scheme of &quot;Capacity Building for Service Providers (CBSP)&quot;. In this scheme, financial assistance is provided to selected institutes to conduct Skill Development Programmes. The program is aimed at targeting persons with not much means and in need to acquire skills facilitative to employment. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual, Institution. Additionally, the nodal ministry is Ministry Of Tourism.

It falls under the categories: Skills & Employment.

Eligibility criteria include: All Government/Private Institutes/organisations/entities are eligible.
