The name of this welfare scheme is Incentive for the SC Widow Re-Marriage. It is also known as ISCWR. This scheme is aimed at encouraging widows to remarry by providing financial assistance to lead a normal life. The main objective of the scheme is to promote widow remarriage and rehabilitate widows by providing financial assistance. This scheme brings in a new dimension of social change among the widows.
Under this scheme, the Social Welfare Department, Government of Karnataka will give incentives to the remarried widows belonging to the SC Community. The incentive is provided only to those who have submitted applications within 1 year of marriage. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Karnataka. The implementing agency is Social Welfare Department. Additionally, the nodal department is Social Welfare Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: If a widow from the Scheduled Caste community remarries a male from another caste, she will receive an incentive of ₹3 lakh.
If the beneficiary has already received an incentive under the simple marriage, she will be eligible for ₹50 lakh under this scheme.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a resident of Karnataka.
   The applicant should be a widow.
   The widow should belong to the Scheduled Caste community.
   There is no annual income limit for this scheme.
   Incentives will be provided only to those who submit their application within one year of marriage.
   There are no caste/religion restrictions for the spouse.

Note: The couple should reside in Karnataka. The application process is as follows: Step 1: First go to the official website.
Step 2: Using your Aadhaar Card, do the registration and provide all the required information.
Step 3: Upload all the relevant documents.
Step 4: After Registration, an SMS will be sent to the applicant and she can print the acknowledgement form as proof of application.
Step 5: Social Welfare department will do spot verification to verify the details provided by the applicant and process the application further.
Step 6: After successful spot verification by the department, the incentive will be credited to the registered bank account of the applicant.
