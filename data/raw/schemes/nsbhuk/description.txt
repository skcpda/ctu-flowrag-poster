The name of this welfare scheme is Nirmaan Shramik  ke Bachchon Hetu  Utkrasht Khel Protsaahan Yojana. It is also known as NSBHUK. Labor Department of Chhattisgarh State started this scheme &amp;amp;quot;Nirmaan Shramik  Ke Bachchon Hetu  Utkrasht Khel Protsaahan Yojana&amp;amp;quot; for Construction Workers on 23 May 2017. The main objective of the scheme to provide financial to encourage the studdents for sports.
&amp;lt;br&amp;gt;

&amp;lt;br&amp;gt; The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Incentive amount will be provided to the children who perform excellently at the district / division / state / national level in all recognized sports as follows Amount at district level: ₹ 1,000/Amount at divisional level: ₹ 2,000/Amount at state level: ₹ 5,000/Amount at National level : ₹ 50,000/

Eligibility criteria include: Benefits will only be provided to children of construction workers who are registered in any district within the state
The benefit of the scheme will be given only to those children whose excellence is recognized by the Sports Department. The application process is as follows: All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Bhavan Evan Koi Sannirmaan&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Yojana&quot;
   What do you want to do : &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Select your district name and provide old/new registration number and click next.
Select the scheme name. 
Fill out the online with required details.
