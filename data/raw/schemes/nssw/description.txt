The name of this welfare scheme is New Swarnima Scheme For Women. It is also known as NSSW. ##### A term loan scheme by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment for women entrepreneurs from backward classes to obtain a loan of up to ₹2,00,000/- @ 5% per annum, thereby providing them social &amp; financial security. The scheme is introduced by National Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (NBCFDC) and implemented by State Channelising Agencies (SCAs) which act as the nodal agency. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. The implementing agency is State Channelising Agencies (SCAs) Of National Backward Classes Finance & Development Corporation (NBCFDC). Additionally, the nodal ministry is Ministry Of Social Justice and Empowerment.

It falls under the categories: Banking,Financial Services and Insurance, Business & Entrepreneurship, Social welfare & Empowerment, Women and Child.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant must be a FEMALE.The age of the applicant must be between 18 and 55 years.The applicant must be an EntrepreneurThe Total Annual Family Income of the Applicant must be Less than ₹ 3 Lakh per Annum For more information, please refer to: Entrepreneurial Schemes Of NBCFDC: https://socialjustice.gov.in/schemes/13 Scheme Guidelines By NBCFDC: https://nbcfdc.gov.in/loan-scheme-description/2/en#:~:text=The%20target%20group%20of%20the,3.00%20Lakh Tags associated with the scheme include: Backward Class, Business, Empowerment, Entrepreneur, Financial Assistance, Women.
