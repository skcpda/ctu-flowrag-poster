The name of this welfare scheme is Medhavi Chhatr/Chhatra Shiksha Protsahan Yojana. It is also known as MCCSPY. Chhattisgarh &quot;Medhaavee Chhaatr/ Chhaatra Shiksha Protsaahan Yojana&quot; has been started by the Government of Chhattisgarh and operated by the Labor Department of Chhattisgarh. Benefits will be provided to applicants studying in 10th, 12th, graduation, post graduation, and some selected courses. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.
