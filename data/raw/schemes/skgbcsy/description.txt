The name of this welfare scheme is Safai Karmakar Gambhir Bimari Chikitsa Sahayata Yojana. It is also known as SKGBCSY. Labor Department of Chhattisgarh State started this scheme &amp;amp;quot;Safai Karmakar Gambhir Bimari Chikitsa Sahayata Yojana&amp;amp;quot; for unorganized worker on 28 April 2012. The main objective of the scheme to provide the financial assistance for medical treatment. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Registered unorganized workers who are suffering from serious illness will be provided medical assistance of â‚¹ 50,000/for their treatment.
Serious diseases will include kidney, cancer, sickle cell anemia, heart disease, AIDS and paralysis.

Eligibility criteria include: To receive the benefits of this scheme, unorganized workers must be registered with the State Board for at least 90 days.
This scheme is open to unorganized workers between the ages of 18 and The unorganized worker must be registered as a beneficiary under Section 10 of the Act.
Each unorganized worker can only receive the benefits of this scheme once.
If the unorganized worker is receiving benefits under another parallel scheme of the State Government, they will not be eligible for the benefits of this scheme The application process is as follows: Unregistered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details
