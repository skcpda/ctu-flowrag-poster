The name of this welfare scheme is Mukhyamantri Nirman Shramikon Ke Bachon Hetu Nihshulk Coaching Sahaayata Yojana. It is also known as MNSKBHNCSY. The Labor Department of Chhattisgarh introduced the &quot;Mukhyamantri Nirman Shramikon Ke Bachon Hetu Nihshulk Coaching Sahaayata Yojana&quot; on March 1, 2023, through the &quot;Building and Construction Workers Welfare Board.&quot; Under this scheme, free coaching assistance will be provided to the children of registered workers. Only the first two children of the worker will be eligible for this scheme. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. The implementing agency is Building And Construction Workers Welfare Board. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Free coaching assistance will be provided to the children of registered workers.



Note: Construction workers and their children registered under the Board are allowed to appear for competitive examinations as per educational qualification like PSC (Public Service Commission), CG VYAPAM (Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board), SSC (Staff Selection Commission), IBPS (Banking), RAILWAY (Railway). ), There is a provision to provide free coaching through selected coaching institutes for POLICE ENTRANCE (Police Recruitment Examination).

Eligibility criteria include: The worker should be registered under The Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board.
The registered worker should have at least one year of regular membership.
The first two children of the worker are eligible for the scheme. The application process is as follows: Registration of a Construction Worker Under  CBOCWWB
