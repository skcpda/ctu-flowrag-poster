The name of this welfare scheme is Organizing Block Demonstration on Polythene Mulch Technology in Groundnut. It is also known as OBDOPMTIG. The scheme &amp;quot;Organizing Block Demonstration on Polythene Mulch Technology in Groundnut&amp;quot; by the Agriculture-Farmers Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu, is designed to support farmers by providing ₹4,000/- for input costs and an additional ₹4,000/- to cover land with polythene sheets per hectare. It aims to encourage the use of advanced agricultural technologies to enhance crop yields, specifically in groundnut farming.
&lt;br&gt;

&lt;br&gt; The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Agriculture Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Financial Assistance: An assistance of  ₹4,000/towards the cost of inputs to organize the demonstration and  ₹4,000/to cover the land with Polythene sheet per hectare will be provided.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Farmer.
  The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
  The beneficiary should produce and supply Foundation and Certified Class seeds to the Department. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Collect the application form from the nearest Agricultural Office.
Step 2: Fill out the application form with the required details and attach mandatory documents (selfattested, if required). 
Step 3: Submit the form to the concerned office:
&gt; Village Level: Submit the application to the Assistant Agricultural Officer.

&gt; Block Level: Submit to the Agricultural Officer or Deputy Agricultural Officer.

&gt; District Level: Submit to the Joint Director of Agriculture. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://www.tn.gov.in/scheme/data_view/7614 Tags associated with the scheme include: Agriculture, Block Demonstration, Farmer, Groundnut, Polythene Mulch Technology.
