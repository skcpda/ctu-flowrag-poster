Eligibility criteria include: All the farmers of Chhattisgarh state who have sold paddy to the government after cultivating paddy in the year Those forest rights holders who had sold paddy to the government last year now want to plant trees instead of paddy on their land.
Forest rights holders who want to plant trees on their land afresh.
