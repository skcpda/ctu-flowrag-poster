The name of this welfare scheme is NAMO E-Tablet Scheme. It is also known as NETS. Detailed 
Under the Gujarat Namo Tablet Yojana, tablets are provided to students studying in colleges and universities in the state. . The tablets are given to students for free and are preloaded with educational content, including textbooks, e-books, and video lectures, to facilitate their studies.
The Gujarat Namo Tablet Yojana is part of the state government&amp;#39;s efforts to promote digital learning and reduce the digital divide among students from different socioeconomic backgrounds. The tablets distributed under the scheme are equipped with 4G connectivity and have a screen size of 7 inches. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Gujarat. The implementing agency is Directorate Of Technical Education, Government Of Gujarat. Additionally, the nodal department is Education Department.

It falls under the categories: Education & Learning.

Eligibility criteria include: Eligibility 

The students should have passed the 12th examination from a recognized institution and board in Gujarat. 
He or she must be in the first year of college.
