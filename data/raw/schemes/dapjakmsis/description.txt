The name of this welfare scheme is Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Meritorious Students Incentive Scheme (Class 12th Passed). It is also known as DAPJAKMSIS. The scheme &amp;quot;Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Meritorious Students Incentive Scheme&amp;quot; was launched by the School Education Department, Government of Madhya Pradesh, to motivate students from lower economic backgrounds and support them economically. Under this scheme, a cash award of ₹5,000/- is given to the students who have obtained the highest marks in the 12th class examination in government schools within their district. To be eligible for this benefit, the annual income of the student&amp;#39;s parents must be less than ₹1,00,000/-. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Madhya Pradesh. The implementing agency is School Education Department, Government Of Madhya Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is School Education Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Under this scheme, students who achieve the highest marks in the 12th class examination in government schools within their district will receive ₹5,000/.

Note: The scholarship is sent directly to the student&amp;#39;s bank account.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant must be a permanent resident of Madhya Pradesh.
   The student must have achieved the highest marks in their district in the Class 12 examination.
   The student must have passed their Class 12 examination from a government school in Madhya Pradesh (Government Higher Secondary School).
   The student&amp;#39;s parents&amp;#39; income from all sources should be less than ₹1,00,000/per year. The application process is as follows: To avail of the benefits under this scheme, eligible students can contact the Principal of their respective government schools/institutions.

Note: The student&amp;#39;s profile is entered on the education portal https://shikshaportal.mp.gov.in/ by the teacher of the educational institution as per the record/certificate provided by the student.
