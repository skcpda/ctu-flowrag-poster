The name of this welfare scheme is Incentive For The Simple Marriage. It is also known as Iftsm. The Simple Marriage scheme is a government initiative by the Social Welfare Department of Karnataka that offers incentives to couples belonging to the SC community who opt for a simple marriage ceremony. The scheme aims to promote the concept of simple marriages and provide financial assistance to eligible couples.


Under this scheme, the Social Welfare Department of Karnataka will provide incentives to Simple Married Couples belonging to the SC Community who have submitted their applications within 1 year of marriage. Only couples who got married on or after 11-08-2015 are eligible for the incentive, which is set at Rs. 50,000.00.


To be eligible for the scheme, the applicant must be a resident of Karnataka state, belong to the SC community, and have a family income from all sources that does not exceed Rs. 2 lakhs per annum. It is important to note that the scheme is applicable within 1 year of marriage It is specifically implemented in Karnataka. Additionally, the nodal department is Social Welfare Department.

It falls under the categories: Social welfare & Empowerment.

Eligibility criteria include: Karnataka State Resident: The applicant must be a resident of Karnataka state.
SC Community: The applicant must belong to the SC community.
Family Income: The family income from all sources should not exceed Rs. 2 lakhs per annum.
Time Limit: The scheme is applicable only within 1 year of the marriage.
