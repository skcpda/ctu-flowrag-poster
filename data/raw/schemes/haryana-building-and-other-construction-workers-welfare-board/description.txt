The name of this welfare scheme is Family Pension (HBOCWWB). It is also known as FP(HBOCWWB). The scheme “Family Pension” is implemented by the Haryana Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board (HBOCWWB), Labour Department, Government of Haryana. Under this scheme, on the death of a pensioner worker who is being given pension by the Haryana Building &amp;amp; Other Construction Workers Welfare Board, half of his/her pension is given to his/her spouse (as the case may be). The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Haryana. The implementing agency is Haryana Building & Other Construction Workers Welfare Board. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: After the death of the workerpensioner, half of the pension amount will be given to the dependent wife/husband as family pension every month.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be the spouse of the deceased worker.
   The deceased worker should have been registered with the Haryana Building &amp;amp; Other Construction Workers Welfare Board.
   The registered deceased worker must have been getting the pension from the Board. The application process is as follows: Apply for Scheme: Registration Process on AntyodayaSARAL Portal:
Step 01: To avail of the benefits of the scheme, the applicant needs to visit the Official Portal AntyodayaSARAL Portal
Step 02: If the applicant is not registered on the portal, then he/she gets registered on the portal. 
Step 03: For registration, click on “New User/Register Here” and fill in all the mandatory details i.e. Name, Email ID, Mobile Number &amp;amp; password. Click on ‘Submit’.
Step 04: Applicants will receive the login ID on their mobile number.
&gt; Login to Apply for the Scheme:

Step 01: Visit the Official Portal AntyodayaSARAL Portal
Step 02: The “Sign in here” option is available on the right side of the screen. Applicant needs to fill in their credentials and click on the ‘Login’ button.
Step 03: In the opened window, click on “Scheme/Services list” and a list of schemes will appear on the screen.
Step 04: Now, select the scheme and click on “Apply for Service/Scheme”.
Step 05: Fill in all the details asked in the online application form and submit the application form.
