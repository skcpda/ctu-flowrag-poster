The name of this welfare scheme is Quality Paddy Seed Distribution. It is also known as QPSD. The scheme “Quality Paddy Seed Distribution”, introduced on 22nd August 2011 by the Agriculture-Farmers Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu, offers a subsidy of ₹5/- per kg on certified paddy seeds. Farmers can avail this benefit when purchasing seeds from Agricultural Extension Centers. This initiative helps reduce the cost burden on farmers, encouraging them to use high-quality paddy seeds for improved crop yields. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Agriculture Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Financial Assistance: ₹5/per kg subsidy on certified paddy seeds.
&lt;br&gt;

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Farmer.
  The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
  The beneficiary must purchase certified paddy seeds from Agricultural Extension Centers. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Collect the application form from the nearest Agricultural Office.
Step 2: Fill out the application form with the required details and attach mandatory documents (selfattested, if required). 
Step 3: Submit the form to the concerned office:
&gt; Village Level: Submit the application to the Assistant Agricultural Officer.

&gt; Block Level: Submit to the Agricultural Officer or Deputy Agricultural Officer.

&gt; District Level: Submit to the Joint Director of Agriculture. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://www.tn.gov.in/scheme/data_view/19244 Tags associated with the scheme include: Agriculture, Farmer, Paddy, Seed Production.
