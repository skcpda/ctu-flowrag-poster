The name of this welfare scheme is Transgender Pension Scheme. It is also known as TPS. The Transgender Pension Scheme is a welfare initiative launched by the Government of Himachal Pradesh for the upliftment and support of transgender persons. The scheme provides a monthly pension of Rs. 650 to transgender persons who are above 18 years of age and belong to the BPL (Below Poverty Line) category. A monthly pension of Rs. 1200 is provided to transgender persons who are above 80 years or 70% disability. The main objective of this scheme is to provide financial assistance to transgender persons and ensure their social security and protection. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Himachal Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is Department of Social Justice and Empowerment.

It falls under the categories: Social welfare & Empowerment.

Eligibility criteria include: •	The applicant must be a resident of Himachal Pradesh.
•	The applicant must be above 18 years of age.
•	The applicant must belong to the BPL category.
•	The applicant must have a transgender identity certificate issued by the District Magistrate or SubDivisional Magistrate.
