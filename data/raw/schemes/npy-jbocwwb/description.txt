The name of this welfare scheme is Nishaktata Pension Yojana (JBOCWWB). It is also known as NPY-JBOCWWB. &quot;Nishaktata Pension Yojana (JBOCWWB)&quot; is a scheme by the Department of Labour, Employment, Training &amp; Skill Development Govt of Jharkhand. This is a 100% State Sponsored Scheme. Only the residents/domicile of the state of Jharkhand can apply to this scheme. Applications for this scheme are accepted online through the official website of the department. The scheme provides a pension of â‚¹ 1,000/- per month to the registered beneficiary who is permanently disabled due to paralysis/leprosy/tuberculosis/accident. It is specifically implemented in Jharkhand. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour, Employment, Training and Skill Department.

It falls under the categories: Banking,Financial Services and Insurance, Social welfare & Empowerment.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a domicile in the state of Jharkhand.
The applicant should be between 1860 years of age.
The applicant should be a Registered Worker with the &#39;Jharkhand Building and Other Construction Worker Welfare Board (JBOCWW Board)&#39;.
The applicant should be permanently disabled due to paralysis/leprosy/tuberculosis/accidents.
