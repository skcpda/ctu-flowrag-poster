The name of this welfare scheme is Internship Scheme For Students Of Counselling/ Psychology/ Social Work/ Home Science Etc. It is also known as ISSCPSWHSE. This is an Internship Scheme by the Directorate of Women and Child Development, Government of Goa. The objective of this scheme is to offer aspiring postgraduate students an opportunity for internship facilities to be trained as qualified interns in state-run private children&#39;s and shelter homes who can be offered hands-on job training experience. The scheme is open to any student who has passed out from any of the recognised universities with a Master&#39;s Degree in Social Service/ Social Work/ Sociology/ Psychology/ Home Science/ Post Graduate Diploma in Counselling etc.


> Number

The total number of slots for interns per year shall be fixed at 15, extendable upto 20.


> Duration

The period of internship shall be for a maximum of twelve months. 


> Location

The selected interns will have to undergo training and work at the following places:

1. State-Run Children&#39;s Home.
1. Protective Home for Women.
1. Juvenile Justice Boards.
1. Child Welfare Committees.
1. At any other Children&#39;s Home, Shelter Homes etc recognized by the Government. 



> Reporting

The Interns shall work as per directions and under total supervision of the officers in charge at the respective place of deployment during the period of internship or when deployed for field work at any place for preparation of Probation Officers&#39; reports or for Home study report inquiries etc. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Goa. The implementing agency is Directorate Of Women And Child Development, Government Of Goa. Additionally, the nodal department is Women and Child Development Department.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should have passed out from any of the recognised universities with a Master&#39;s Degree.
The Master&#39;s Degree should have been in Social Service/ Social Work/ Sociology/ Psychology/ Home Science/ Post Graduate Diploma in Counselling etc.
The applicant should have passed out as a postgraduate within three years prior to applying for the internship.
The applicant should have obtained at least 50% marks in aggregate in the final year of the postgraduation.
