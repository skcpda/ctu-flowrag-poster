The name of this welfare scheme is Pension to Widows and Destitute Women. It is also known as PTWADW. The &amp;quot;Pension to Widows and Destitute Women&amp;quot; scheme was introduced by the Government of Haryana in the financial year 1980-81. The aim of the scheme is to provide social security to women who cannot sustain themselves and need financial assistance. Under this scheme, widows and destitute women aged 18 years and above are eligible for a pension if they are domiciles of Haryana and have been residing in the state for at least one year at the time of application submission. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Haryana. Additionally, the nodal department is Government of Haryana.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Under this scheme, a pension of ₹2,500/per month is given to widows and destitute women.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a permanent resident of Haryana.
The applicant should meet one of the following conditions:
•	Be a widow.
•	Be a destitute woman without a husband, parents, or children.
•	Be a destitute woman due to desertion or the physical/mental incapacity of her husband (for married women) or parents (for other women).
The applicant should be 18 years of age or older.
The applicant&#39;s annual income from all sources should be less than ₹2,00,000/. The application process is as follows: Step 1: For applying the widow pension scheme, the applicant has to furnish an application form in the prescribed format.
Step 2: The applicant has to affix a recent passportsize photograph on the application.
Step 3: Approach the concerned Social Justice Department (SJD) in the locality, submit the duly filled application form along with all required documents.
Step 4: Concerned SJD authority will verify the application form and other documents. Once the concerned authority approves the application, the applicant will be provided with a beneficiary ID.
Note 1: The same ID will also send through the registered mobile number. Keep it safe for future reference.
Note 2: All eligible beneficiaries will start to receive the allowance pension every month. The amount will be directly credited to the beneficiary’s bank account. One can make an application through eDisha Center as well as Atal Seva Kendra.
