The name of this welfare scheme is Uniform Assistance To SC Students. It is also known as UASCS. Objective 
- Main Objective is to provide economic support and motivation for education. Under this scheme, assistance is given to Students studying in classes 1 to 8. 
- Through this scheme, the Gujarat government promotes primary education. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Gujarat. The implementing agency is District Deputy Director(SCW) Office. Additionally, the nodal department is Social Justice and Empowerment Department.

It falls under the categories: Social welfare & Empowerment.

Eligibility criteria include: The student should be of Scheduled Caste.
Students should be studying in classes 1 to No Income Limit. For more information, please refer to: Details: https://sje.gujarat.gov.in/dscw/schemes/1544?lang=English Tags associated with the scheme include: Financial Assistance, Scheduled Caste, Uniform Assistance.
