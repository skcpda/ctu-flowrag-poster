The name of this welfare scheme is Hari Om Ashram Prerit Senior Scientist Award. It is also known as HOAPSSA. The Hari Om Ashram Prerit Senior Scientist Award was established to commemorate the birth centenary of Pujya Shri Mota, the founder of the Hari Om Ashram, Nadiad. It is awarded to an eminent Indian scientist for their innovative and outstanding contributions to the field of Science and Technology. The award comprises a silver plaque, a citation, and a cash prize of ₹4,00,000/-. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. Additionally, the nodal ministry is Ministry Of Science And Technology and the nodal department is Department of Space.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Award Amount: ₹4,00,000/Recognition: Silver Plaque and Citation

Eligibility criteria include: The nominee should be an Indian citizen.
The nominee should be a scientist. 
The nominee&#39;s contributions must be in Science and Technology. The application process is as follows: Step01: The applicant visits the PRL website and navigate to the &quot;Opportunities&quot; section.
Step02: Click on &quot;Awards Administered By PRL&quot; and select scheme &quot;Hari Om Ashram Prerit Senior Scientist Award&quot; 
Step03: The candidate downloads the prescribed format of the application form. 
Step04: In the application form, fill in all the mandatory fields, and attach copies of all the mandatory documents (selfattest, if required).
Step05: Candidate can email their applications in the prescribed format to: DIRECTOR@PRL.RES.IN with the subject line &quot;Nomination for The Hari Om Ashram Prerit Senior Scientist Award&quot;. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://www.prl.res.in/prl-eng/sr_scientist_award Tags associated with the scheme include: Award, Cash Prize, Scientist.
