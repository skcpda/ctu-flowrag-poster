The name of this welfare scheme is Shilpi Samridhi Yojana. It is also known as SSYH. &amp;quot;Shilpi Samridhi Yojana&amp;quot; is an economic empowerment scheme by the Haryana Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation, Government of Haryana. Through this scheme, loans/benefits are provided to the eligible families of the scheduled caste artisans to pursue various economic development activities for the betterment of their economic status. In order to be eligible, the artisan should be in the age group of 18 to 45 years. He/She should not be a defaulter of the Corporation/bank. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Haryana. Additionally, the nodal department is Welfare of Scheduled Caste & Backward Classes Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Loan for various economic development schemes having unit cost up to ₹50,000/.
Margin Money at 10% of the project cost.
Subsidy at 50% of the total project cost, subject to a maximum of ₹10,000/.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a permanent resident of Haryana.
  The applicant should belong to the Scheduled Caste category.
  The applicant&amp;#39;s annual family income should not exceed ₹3,00,000/.
  The applicant should be in the age group of 18 to 45 years.
  The applicant should have adequate knowledge and experience of the scheme.


  The applicant should not have misused the previous loan obtained from the Corporation.
  The applicant should not have any other similar unit.
  The applicant should not be a defaulter of the Corporation/banks. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Visit the official website of the &amp;amp;amp;amp;quot;Antyodaya SARAL&amp;amp;amp;amp;quot; Portal.
Step 2: In the &amp;amp;amp;amp;quot;SIGN IN HERE&amp;amp;amp;amp;quot; section, click &amp;amp;amp;amp;quot;New user? Register here&amp;amp;amp;amp;quot;.
Step 3: The registration form will open in a new window of the browser. Fill in all the mandatory fields of the form. Create a strong password. Fill in the captcha code and click &amp;amp;amp;amp;quot;Submit&amp;amp;amp;amp;quot;.
Step 4: An OTP will be sent to the email ID and the Mobile Number provided in the registration form.
