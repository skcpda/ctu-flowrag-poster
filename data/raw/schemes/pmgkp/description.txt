The name of this welfare scheme is Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package : Insurance Scheme For Health Workers Fighting COVID-19. It is also known as PMGKP. An Insurance scheme from Ministry of Health and Family Welfare for health workers who Lost their life due to COVID19 or accidental death on account of COVID-19 related duty.




This accident insurance scheme covers;

- Loss of life due to COVID19, and
- Accidental death on account of COVID-19 related duty.





#### Beneficiary 



- Public healthcare providers including community health workers, who may have to be indirect contact and care of COVID-19 patients and who may be at risk of being impacted by this.






- Private hospital staff and retired /volunteer /local urban bodies/ contracted /daily wage /ad hoc/outsourced staff requisitioned by States/ Central hospitals/ autonomous hospitals of Central/ States/UTs, AIIMS and INIs/ hospital of Central Ministries can also be drafted for COVID 19 related responsibilities. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. Additionally, the nodal ministry is Ministry Of Health & Family Welfare.

It falls under the categories: Health & Wellness.

Eligibility criteria include: Public healthcare providers including community health workers, who may have to be in direct contact and care of COVID19 case and who may be at risk of being impacted by this.




Private hospital staff and retired /volunteer /local urban bodies/ contracted /daily wage /adhoc/outsourced staff requisitioned by States/ Central hospitals/ autonomous hospitals of Central/ States/UTs, AIIMS and INIs/ hospital of Central Ministries can also be drafted for COVID 19 related responsibilities.
