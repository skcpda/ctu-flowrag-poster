The name of this welfare scheme is Mukhyamantri Asangathit Karmakar Ijjat Monthly Season Ticket Card Yojana. It is also known as MAKIMSTC. Labor Department of Chhattisgarh State started this scheme &amp;amp;quot;Mukhyamantri Asangathit Karmakar Ijjat Monthly Season Ticket Card Yojana&amp;amp;quot; for unorganized worker on 19 June 2017. The main objective of the scheme to empower unorganized worker by providing travel pass for train. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: > Train pass for daily travel up to a maximum of 150 kilometers.

Eligibility criteria include: Unorganized workers must be registered with the board.
If the Unorganized workers already received benefits from any other similar scheme of the state government, they will not be eligible for the benefits of this scheme. The application process is as follows: Unregistered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Asangathit Shramik Panjikaran&quot;
   What do you want to do :  &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Check the eligibility criteria for registration in Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board and click next. 
Fill out the online &quot;Registration form&quot;.
Submit.



Already Registered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Yojana&quot;
   What do you want to do :  &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Select your district name and provide old/new registration number and click next.
Select the scheme name. 
Fill out the online with required details. 
Submit.
