The name of this welfare scheme is Organizing Block Demonstration Through Department And Also Through TNAU. It is also known as OBDTDAATTNAU. The scheme “Organizing Block Demonstration Through Department And Also Through TNAU” by the Agriculture-Farmers Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu is designed to promote maize cultivation through block demonstrations conducted by the Department of Agriculture and Tamil Nadu Agricultural University (TNAU). Farmers in select districts of Tamil Nadu can participate. It provides ₹4,000/- per demonstration for 1-hectare plots. Special focus is given to small, marginal, SC/ST, and women farmers. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Agriculture Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Financial Assistance: ₹4,000/per demonstration of 1 Hectare plot each.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Farmer.
  The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
  The beneficiary should grow maize in the following districts: Coimbatore, Dindigul, Erode, Dharmapuri, Krishnagiri, Namakkal, Perambalur, Virudhunagar, Theni, Villupuram, Vellore, Salem, Pudukkottai, Madurai, Sivagangai, Trichy, Tirunelveli, and Thoothukudi.

&lt;br&gt; The application process is as follows: Step 1: Collect the application form from the nearest Agricultural Office.
Step 2: Fill out the application form with the required details and attach mandatory documents (selfattested, if required). 
Step 3: Submit the form to the concerned office:
&gt; Village Level: Submit the application to the Assistant Agricultural Officer.

&gt; Block Level: Submit to the Agricultural Officer or Deputy Agricultural Officer.

&gt; District Level: Submit to the Joint Director of Agriculture. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://www.tn.gov.in/scheme/data_view/6854 Tags associated with the scheme include: Agriculture, Block Demonstration, Farmer, Maize.
