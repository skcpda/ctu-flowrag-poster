The name of this welfare scheme is Super IAS 500 Scholarship Manipur. It is also known as ALSIAS. Super IAS 500 Scholarship Manipur is a scholarship initiative launched by the Manipur government to support and encourage meritorious and brilliant IAS aspirants in the state. The scholarship will provide offline coaching facilities by renowned IAS mentors from the Alternative Learning System (ALS), Delhi, and will be held at the State Academy Training (SAT) at Takyelpat with state government funding. The first phase of the coaching program began on November 8, 2021, and will last until April 2022. The scholarship aims to help candidates avoid expensive coaching programs outside the state and provide them with an opportunity to receive quality civil service training within the state It is specifically implemented in Manipur. Additionally, the nodal department is General Administration Department.

It falls under the categories: Education & Learning.

Eligibility criteria include: The candidate must be a domicile of India.
He/She must be a permanent resident of the state of Manipur.
The applicant must be a civil service student.
The candidate must be a Meritorious and Brilliant IAS aspirant of Manipur.
