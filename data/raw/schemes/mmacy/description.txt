The name of this welfare scheme is Mukhyamantri Alpsankhyak Chhatravas Yojana. It is also known as MMACY. The &amp;quot;Mukhyamantri Alpsankhyak Chhatravas Yojana&amp;quot; is a scheme initiated by the Minority Welfare Department, Government of Bihar. Under this scheme, students belonging to minority communities who are studying in a recognized institution, school, college, university, or technical institution in their respective districts and residing in minority hostels are provided a monthly stipend of ₹1,000/-. Additionally, these students receive 9 kg of rice and 6 kg of wheat every month, while residing in hostels run by the Minority Welfare Department, Government of Bihar. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Bihar. The implementing agency is Minority Welfare Department, Government Of Bihar. Additionally, the nodal department is Minority Welfare Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Under this scheme, students from minority communities who are studying and residing in hostels run by the Minority Welfare Department, Government of Bihar, will receive the following benefits:

  A monthly stipend of ₹1,000/.
  Provision of essential facilities such as cots, mattresses, bed sheets, tables, chairs for studying, as well as cooking utensils and kitchen facilities. 
  Monthly distribution of 9 kg of rice and 6 kg of wheat to each student.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a native of Bihar.
   The applicant should be a student.
   The student should belong to a minority community.
   The student should be enrolled in a recognized institution, school, college, university, or technical institution in their respective district.
   The student should be pursuing a course that is recognized by the Government.
   The student should reside in a hostel operated by the Minority Welfare Department, Government of Bihar, while pursuing their studies. The application process is as follows: &gt; Registration Process:

Step 1: Go to the Ekalyan Portal.
Step 2: Review the guidelines and select &amp;quot;Create Account.&amp;quot;
Step 3: Complete the registration form with all required details (marked with ()).
Step 4: Click &amp;quot;Register&amp;quot; to finalize the process.
Step 5: You will receive a confirmation message with your User ID and password displayed on the screen. 
&gt; Apply for Scheme:

Step 1: Login to the EKalyan Portal using your User ID and password from registration.
Step 2: Enter your personal, academic, and other relevant details.
Step 3: Choose the district(s) for which you are applying, noting that you can select multiple hostels within that district.
Step 4: Click &amp;quot;Save and Continue&amp;quot; to proceed.
Step 5: Complete the application by uploading all required documents and clicking &amp;quot;Submit.&amp;quot;
Note 1: Once submitted, the form cannot be modified.
Note 2: The Minority Welfare Department will seek the student list from the Principal of the minority hostel where the students stay.
