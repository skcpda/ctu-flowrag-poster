The name of this welfare scheme is Old Age Home (Sahara). It is also known as OAH. Under this scheme construction and operation of the old age home &amp;quot;Sahara&amp;quot; will be included in which destitute Housing, clothes, food, medical facilities, etc. are made available for qualitative improvement in the standard of living of poor and poor elders, for cooperation and proper care. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Bihar. Additionally, the nodal department is Social Welfare Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Construction and operation of old age home &amp;quot;Sahara&amp;quot;
   These old age homes also include housing, clothes, food, medical facilities etc. 
   Facilities will be available for qualitative improvement in the standard of living, cooperation and proper care of destitute and poor old people of the state.

Eligibility criteria include: Applicants should come under the below poverty line ( BPL) category (or)
Annual Income should be below Rs. 60,000/ The application process is as follows: Applicants can apply through the application in plain paper to the District Social Security Cell.
The committee constituted under the chairmanship of the District Magistrate will approve the proposal.
