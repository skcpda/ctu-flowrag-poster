The name of this welfare scheme is Grant for the Purchase of Tools/ Protective Gear for the Construction Workers. It is also known as GFTPOTPGFTCW. The &amp;quot;Grant for the Purchase of Tools/ Protective Gear for the Construction Workers&amp;quot; by the Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board, Labour Department, U.T. Administration of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu, is a welfare scheme for the registered workers of the board. Under this scheme, registered beneficiaries of the board shall be provided with financial assistance for purchase of tools. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in The Dadra And Nagar Haveli And Daman And Diu. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour & Employment Department, Dadra and Nagar Haveli.

The scheme provides the following benefits: An amount not exceeding ₹10,000/.

Note: The benefit shall be allowed for once in a three years.

Eligibility criteria include: &gt; For Registration as a Building/ Construction Worker:


  The applicant should be a resident of U.T. Administration of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu.
  The applicant should be a Building/ Construction Worker.
  The applicant should be between 18 and 60 years.
  The applicant should have engaged in any building or other construction work for at least 90 days in a given year.

&gt; For the Application of the Welfare Scheme:


  Registered member Under the Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board of U.T. Administration of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu.
  The applicant should have completed six months of the membership. The application process is as follows: &gt; Registration Process as a Building/ Construction Worker:

Step 1: Application for registration is to be made in the prescribed form and is to be accompanied by prescribed documents and a fee of not more than ₹50/.
Step 2: Application for registration has to be made to the officer authorized by the Board on this behalf.
&gt; Application Process of the Welfare Scheme:

Step 1: Any registered worker can make an application in the prescribed FormIII (Refer Page No: 8)  along with the mandatory documents  (self attested, if required).
Step 2: Application shall be submitted to the Member Secretary (BOCWWB) for this benefit.
&gt; Check Beneficiary Details: Visit the official website and enter the “BOCW Registration No.” of the concerned beneficiary in the Search box.
