The name of this welfare scheme is Pension Yojana (JBOCWWB). It is also known as PY-JBOCWWB. &amp;quot;Pension Yojana (JBOCWWB)&amp;quot; is a scheme by the Department of Labour, Employment, Training &amp;amp; Skill Development Govt of Jharkhand. This is a 100% State Sponsored Scheme. Only the residents/domicile of the state of Jharkhand can apply to this scheme. Applications for this scheme are accepted online through the official website of the department. The scheme provides pension of â‚¹ 1,000/- per month to those who have contributed to the Jharkhand Building and Other Construction Worker Welfare Board (JBOCWW Board) for at least 30 years. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Jharkhand. The implementing agency is Jharkhand Building And Other Construction Worker Welfare Board (JBOCWW Board). Additionally, the nodal department is Labour, Employment, Training and Skill Department.

It falls under the categories: Banking,Financial Services and Insurance.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a domicile in the state of Jharkhand.
The applicant should have been a Worker registered with the Jharkhand Building and Other Construction Worker Welfare Board (JBOCWW Board).
The applicant should be between 1860 years of age.
The applicant should be at least 60 years of age in order to receive the pension.
The applicant should have engaged in construction work such as masons, carpenters, electricians, porters, painters, etc.
The registered worker should have made continuous contributions for 30 years.
