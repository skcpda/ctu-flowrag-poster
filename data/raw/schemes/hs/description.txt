The name of this welfare scheme is House Subsidy Scheme. It is also known as HS. The housing subsidy scheme was launched by the Government of Himachal Pradesh to provide financial assistance to individuals from marginalized sections for the construction of their own houses. Under this scheme, a subsidy of Rs. 1,30,000/- is provided to persons from Scheduled Castes, Other Backward Classes, Minority Communities, Single Women, or handicapped for the construction of their houses. This scheme aims to improve the living conditions and overall quality of life of individuals from these sections. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Family. It is specifically implemented in Himachal Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is Department of Social Justice and Empowerment.

It falls under the categories: Housing & Shelter.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a resident of Himachal Pradesh.
The applicant should belong to Scheduled Cast, Other Backward Classes, Minority Community, Single Women, or Handicapped.
