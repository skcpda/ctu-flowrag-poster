The name of this welfare scheme is Scholarship for Fatherless Girls. It is also known as SFG. The &amp;quot;Scholarship for Fatherless Girls&amp;quot; scheme was launched by the School Education Department, Government of Madhya Pradesh, to support the education of girls who have lost their fathers and are studying in government schools. Under this scheme, scholarships ranging from ₹350/- to ₹450/- are provided to eligible girls from Class 1 to Class 12. To be eligible, girls students should not be beneficiaries of any other scholarship and must have passed their previous class. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Madhya Pradesh. The implementing agency is School Education Department, Government Of Madhya Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is School Education Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Under this scheme, the scholarships will be given at the following rates:Class 1 to 5:    ₹350/per year  Class 6 to 8:    ₹400/per year  Class 9 to 12:  ₹450/per year 
Note: The scholarship will be deposited directly into the student&amp;#39;s bank account.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a girl student.

   The girl student should be studying in Class 1 to The girl student should be studying at a government school in Madhya Pradesh.
   The girl student who has lost her father is eligible for benefits under this scheme.
   The girl student should not be a beneficiary of any other scholarship.
   The girl student should have passed the previous class. The application process is as follows: To avail of the benefits under this scheme, eligible girl students can contact the Principal of their respective government schools/institutions.
