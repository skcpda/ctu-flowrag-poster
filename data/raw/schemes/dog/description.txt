The name of this welfare scheme is Distribution of Gypsum. It is also known as DOG. The “Distribution of Gypsum” scheme by the Agriculture-Farmers Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu, provides financial assistance to farmers by subsidizing 50% of the cost of gypsum material and transportation cost. It is targeted at farmers who grow pulse crops, helping them improve soil fertility and increase yield. The scheme includes special provisions for SC/ST farmers (24%) and farm women (20%). The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Agriculture Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: > Financial Assistance: 


50% on the cost of Material.
Transport cost limited to ₹750/per hectare.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Farmer.
  The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
  The beneficiary should grow pulse crops. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Collect the application form from the nearest Agricultural Office.
Step 2: Fill out the application form with the required details and attach mandatory documents (selfattested, if required). 
Step 3: Submit the form to the concerned office:
&gt; Village Level: Submit the application to the Assistant Agricultural Officer.

&gt; Block Level: Submit to the Agricultural Officer or Deputy Agricultural Officer.

&gt; District Level: Submit to the Joint Director of Agriculture.
