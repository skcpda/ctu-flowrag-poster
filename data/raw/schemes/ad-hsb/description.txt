The name of this welfare scheme is Assistance on Death- Haryana Silicosis Board. It is also known as AD-HSB. The &amp;quot;Assistance on Death&amp;quot; scheme, implemented by the Haryana Labour Welfare Board, Government of Haryana, provides financial assistance of ₹1,00,000/- in the event of the death of a Silicosis victim. If the deceased was married, the assistance will be payable to the widow or widower. If the deceased was unmarried, the amount will be given to their nominee. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Family. It is specifically implemented in Haryana. The implementing agency is Haryana Labour Welfare Board, Government Of Haryana. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Under this scheme, in the event of the death of a Silicosis victim, financial assistance of ₹1,00,000/will be provided.

Note 1: If the deceased was married, the assistance will be payable to the widow or widower.
Note 2: If the deceased was unmarried, the financial assistance will be given to the nominee.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be the widow/widower/nominee of the deceased worker.
   The deceased worker should have been suffering from silicosis as confirmed by the Silicosis Diagnosis Board, Haryana.
   The deceased worker should have been employed in a factory or at a construction site. The application process is as follows: Step 01: The applicant may visit the nearest Labour Office and collect the application form. The Application Performa (Annexure VIII.pdf)) may also be downloaded from the Official Website of the Labour Department Haryana.
Step 02: Fill out the application form with all required details, ensuring accuracy and completeness.
Step 03: Attach all necessary documents, including medical certificates, proof of identity, and the certificate of being the widow/widower or nominee, if applicable, etc.
Step 04: Submit the completely filled application form along with supporting documents to the Assistant Director, Industrial Health of your concerned jurisdiction.
Step 05: The Assistant Director will review the application and then forward it to the Office of the Labour CommissionercumWelfare Commissioner, Haryana.
Step 06: Await notification from the Labour Department regarding the approval or any further requirements for your application.
Step 07: Upon approval, the financial assistance will be processed and provided to the applicant as per the scheme&amp;#39;s guidelines. To avail of the benefits of the scheme, the eligible applicant may visit the nearest Antyodaya Kendra and fill up the form.
