The name of this welfare scheme is Chief Ministergi Artistesinggi Tengbang (CMAT): Support For Purchase Of Instruments / Equipment. It is also known as CMAT-SPIE. Musical instruments or equipment have been essential items for the students and artistes to carry out their learning or performance. The scheme &amp;quot;Chief Ministergi Artistesinggi Tengbang (CMAT): Support for Purchase of Instruments / Equipment&amp;quot; by the Department of Art and Culture, Govt. of Manipur aims to support many talented students and budding artistes, who are economically well but in need of instruments/equipment for their learning and performance purpose to help them groom towards professionalism. In every academic year, 300 students will be supported through this scheme. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Artists, Individual. It is specifically implemented in Manipur. The implementing agency is Directorate Of Art & Culture, Government Of Manipur. Additionally, the nodal department is Art and Culture Department.

It falls under the categories: Sports & Culture, Social welfare & Empowerment.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a resident/domicile of the state of Manipur.
The applicant should be a Student at one of the following institutes: Government Dance College, Shree Shree Bal Mukunda Dev Music College, Imphal Art College, Manipur University of Culture, Jawaharlal Nehru Manipur Dance Academy, and Manipur University (Dance, Music, and Fine Art Department).
The applicant should be pursuing a course in one of the art forms.
The applicant should be below 30 years of age (age to be reckoned on 1st April).
