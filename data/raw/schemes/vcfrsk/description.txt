The name of this welfare scheme is Visit of Contract Farming Farmers to the Research Stations in Karnataka. It is also known as VCFRSK. The scheme “Visit of Contract Farming Farmers to the Research Stations in Karnataka” by the Agriculture-Farmers Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu, aims to enhance the agricultural practices of contract farming farmers by enabling visits to research stations in Karnataka. A batch of 50 farmers will be sponsored for a 3-day visit. The scheme focuses on increasing exposure to advanced techniques and research findings to improve farming outcomes. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Agriculture Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Financial Assistance: ₹1,12,500/per batch of 50 farmers for 3 days.Learning Program: Exposure to the latest agricultural research and innovations.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Farmer.
  The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Collect the application form from the nearest Agricultural Office.
Step 2: Fill out the application form with the required details and attach mandatory documents (selfattested, if required). 
Step 3: Submit the form to the concerned office:
&gt; Village Level: Submit the application to the Assistant Agricultural Officer.

&gt; Block Level: Submit to the Agricultural Officer or Deputy Agricultural Officer.

&gt; District Level: Submit to the Joint Director of Agriculture. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://www.tn.gov.in/scheme/data_view/6858 Tags associated with the scheme include: Agriculture, Contract Farming, Farmer, Research Stations Visit.
