The name of this welfare scheme is West Bengal Artisans Financial Benefit Scheme: Support for Digital Marketing. It is also known as WBAFBSSDM. “Support for Digital Marketing” is a sub-scheme under “West Bengal Artisans Financial Benefit Scheme&amp;quot;, launched by the Micro, Small &amp;amp; Medium Enterprise and Textiles Department, Government of West Bengal on 7th March, 2024, and it shall remain in force for a period of five years. The objective of the scheme is to provide access to digital markets and e-commerce, which will create sustainable business opportunities for the artisans. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Artists, Registered Societies. It is specifically implemented in West Bengal. Additionally, the nodal department is Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises and Textiles Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Individual Artist: ₹2000/.Industrial Cooperative Society: ₹10,000/.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be an artisan or belong to any group of artisans of any industrial cooperative society.
The age of the applicant should be between 1860 years.
Applicant should be engaged in traditional Craft/trade with hand tools/equipment/ small machines.
Applicant should be enlisted in the State Portal for artisans &amp; weavers. 
Applicant should be an Indian citizen permanent resident of West Bengal for the past 10 years. The application process is as follows: Applications by individual artisans or by the Cooperative Societies of the artisans are to be submitted at BDO&amp;#39;’s office for rural areas, at SDO’s office for Municipality areas and at the Municipal Corporation Office for the urban areas, as the case may be.
