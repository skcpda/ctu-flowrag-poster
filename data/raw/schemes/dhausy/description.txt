The name of this welfare scheme is Dhobhi Hetu Avashyak Upkaran Sahayata Yojana. It is also known as DHAUSY. The Labor Department of Chhattisgarh introduced the &quot;Dhobhi Hetu Avashyak Upkaran Sahayata Yojana&quot; on January 10, 2018, through the &quot;Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board.&quot; Under this scheme, the washerman is provided with materials worth Rs. 1,500/-, including electronic/coal iron, clothespins, and a washing paddle (Thappi/Kutela). The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. The implementing agency is Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

Eligibility criteria include: Applicant must be registered Unorganized worker of State Social Security Board. 
The applicant should be a Dhobhi.
Applicant age must be between 18 to 60 years. The application process is as follows: Unregistered Applicant :
Step1: All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. 
Step2: On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.
Step3: Enter the following details


  Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
  Select Service : &quot;Asangathit Shramik Panjikaran&quot;
  What do you want to do :  &quot;Aavedan&quot;
  Click next.


Step4: Check the eligibility criteria for registration in Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board and click next. 
Step5: Fill out the online &quot;Registration form&quot;.
Step6: Submit.


> How to Apply

Step1: All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. 
Step2: On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.
Step3:  Enter the following details


  Select : &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.
  Select Service : Scheme
  What do you want to do : &quot;Application&quot;.
  Click Proceed.


Step4: Select your district name and provide old/new registration number and click proceed.
Step5: When you enter the district and registration number, an OTP will be sent to your mobile for verification. 
Step6: After this, a dashboard will open in front of you, containing information about your name, address, age, and caste etc.
Step7: After that, you select your Yojana and upload your important documents and basic information.
Step8: Click &quot;Submit&quot; to register yourself for this scheme.
