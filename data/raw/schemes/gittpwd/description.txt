The name of this welfare scheme is Government Institutions for Teaching and Training of Persons with Disabilities. It is also known as GITTPWD. The scheme &amp;quot;Government Institutions for Teaching and Training of Persons with Disabilities&amp;quot; was implemented by the Social Welfare Department, Government of Chhattisgarh. The objective of the scheme is to arrange for the education and rehabilitation of children with disabilities by providing them free hostel, education-training, food, clothing, and residential facilities. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. The implementing agency is Social Welfare Department, Government Of Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Social Welfare Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Under the scheme, beneficiaries will receive education, preprofessional training, and free hostel facilities in the concerned government institutions.
