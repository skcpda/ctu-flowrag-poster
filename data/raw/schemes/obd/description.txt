The name of this welfare scheme is Organizing Block Demonstration. It is also known as OBD. The “Organizing Block Demonstration” scheme by the Agriculture-Farmers Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu, provides subsidy to promote best agricultural practices to farmers who cultivate seed farms with Pulses crops. The subsidy covers 50% of the input costs or ₹2,000/- per demonstration, whichever is less. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Agriculture Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Financial Assistance: A subsidy of 50% on the cost of inputs required to layout the demonstration of 5 hectare each or ₹2,000/whichever is less.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Farmer.
The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
The beneficiary should cultivate seed farms with Pulses crops. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Collect the application form from the nearest Agricultural Office.
Step 2: Fill out the application form with the required details and attach mandatory documents (selfattested, if required). 
Step 3: Submit the form to the concerned office:
&gt; Village Level: Submit the application to the Assistant Agricultural Officer.

&gt; Block Level: Submit to the Agricultural Officer or Deputy Agricultural Officer.

&gt; District Level: Submit to the Joint Director of Agriculture. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://www.tn.gov.in/scheme/data_view/6902 Tags associated with the scheme include: Agriculture, Block Demonstration, Farmer, Subsidy.
