The name of this welfare scheme is Distribution of Rhizobium Packets-Oilseeds. It is also known as DORPO. The “Distribution of Rhizobium Packets-Oilseeds” scheme by the Agriculture-Farmers Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu, provides a subsidy to farmers on the cost of Rhizobium. It targets farmers who produce and supply Foundation and Certified Class seeds to the Department of Agriculture. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Agriculture Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Quantum of Assistance: 50% on the cost of Rhizobium or ₹100/per ha. whichever is less.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Farmer.
  The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
  The beneficiary should produce and supply Foundation and Certified Class seeds to the Department.

&lt;br&gt; The application process is as follows: Step 1: Collect the application form from the nearest Agricultural Office.
Step 2: Fill out the application form with the required details and attach mandatory documents (selfattested, if required). 
Step 3: Submit the form to the concerned office:
&gt; Village Level: Submit the application to the Assistant Agricultural Officer.

&gt; Block Level: Submit to the Agricultural Officer or Deputy Agricultural Officer.

&gt; District Level: Submit to the Joint Director of Agriculture. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://www.tn.gov.in/scheme/data_view/7616 Tags associated with the scheme include: Agriculture, Farmer, Oilseeds, Rhizobium, Subsidy.
