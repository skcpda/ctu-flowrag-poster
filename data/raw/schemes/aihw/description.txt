The name of this welfare scheme is West Bengal Handloom and Khadi Weavers Financial Benefit Scheme 2024: Assistance to Individual Handloom Weaver. It is also known as AIHW. “Assistance to Individual Handloom Weaver&amp;quot; is a sub-scheme under &amp;quot;West Bengal Handloom and Khadi Weavers Financial Benefit Scheme 2024”, launched by the Micro, Small &amp;amp; Medium Enterprise and Textiles Department, Government of West Bengal on 7th March, 2024, and it shall remain in force for a period of five years. The objective of this sub-scheme is to provide one-time financial assistance to the individual Handloom weavers who are not under the ambit of any Primary Weavers’ Cooperative Societies. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in West Bengal. Additionally, the nodal department is Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises and Textiles Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: ₹5000/per weaver towards repair/renovation of work shed and/or repair/modification of loom &amp; accessories.
₹10,000/will also be provided towards raw material assistance for production of handloom products. To get this benefit, the raw material, i.e. yarn has to be procured through Tantuja.

Eligibility criteria include: Applicant should be an Individual Weavers. The application process is as follows: An eligible PWCs/ Khadi society/ Individual Weaver will apply either in online or in offline mode in the prescribed format (as would be notified by this department) before the Director, Textiles.
