The name of this welfare scheme is Maternity Assistance for the Construction Worker. It is also known as MAFCWS. The &amp;quot;Maternity Assistance for the Construction Workers&amp;quot; by the Sikkim Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board, Labour Department, is a welfare scheme for the registered women workers of the board. Under this scheme, the women employees who are registered beneficiary for a period of one year shall be given a sum as may be determined by the Board from time to time as maternity benefit during the period of maternity. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Sikkim. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

Eligibility criteria include: &gt; For Registration as a Building/ Construction Worker:


  The applicant should be a resident of Sikkim.
  The applicant should be a Building/ Construction Worker.
  The applicant should be between 18 and 60 years.
  The applicant should have served the Board for at least 90 days in a given year.

&gt; For the Application of the Welfare Scheme:


  Registered women member Under the Sikkim Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board.
  Women should be pregnant.
  The applicant shall be eligible to avail the benefits only for their first 2 children. The application process is as follows: &gt; Registration Process as a Building/ Construction Worker:

Step 1: The complete application form along with the required documents is required to be submitted to the Registering Officer of the area.
Step 2: The Registering Officer, after satisfying himself/herself with all the details, may register the worker as a beneficiary under the act and issue an Identity Card in Form XXVIII.
&gt; Application Process of the Welfare Scheme:

Step 1: Upon receiving the Identity Card as a Building/ Construction Worker, the applicant needs to download the application form for the concerned welfare scheme and submit the form along with the mandatory documents to the Secretary or any other Officer authorized by him in the District Labour Office (self attested, if required).
Step 2: Later on, verifying the eligibility of the concerned welfare scheme, the applicant may get the benefits.

  Application form for the Registration as a Beneficiary: Form XXVII (Refer Page No: 153).  Application form for the Maternity Assistance: Form XLI (Refer Page No: 167).
