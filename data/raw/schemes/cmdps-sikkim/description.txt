The name of this welfare scheme is Chief Minister's State Disability Pension Scheme - Sikkim. It is also known as CMDPS-SIKKIM. The scheme “Chief Minister&amp;#39;s State Disability Pension Scheme (CMDPS)” was started by the Women &amp;amp; Child Welfare Department, Govt. of Sikkim to provide financial assistance to the differently abled person who has a disability of 40% and above. This scheme is 100% State Innovative Scheme and the beneficiary will receive a monthly pension of ₹ 1500/-. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Sikkim. The implementing agency is Women & Child Welfare Department, Govt. Of Sikkim. Additionally, the nodal department is Women & Child Welfare Department.

It falls under the categories: Social welfare & Empowerment, Banking,Financial Services and Insurance.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a resident of the state of Sikkim.
The applicant with 40% and above disability is eligible for the scheme.
The applicant should belong to Below Poverty Line (BPL) household.
The applicant should not be covered under Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme (IGNDPS).
The applicant should have opened the Bank Account under “Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana”. For more information, please refer to: Website Link: https://pensionscheme.sikkim.gov.in Tags associated with the scheme include: Below Poverty Line, Direct Benefit Transfer, Disability, Pension.
