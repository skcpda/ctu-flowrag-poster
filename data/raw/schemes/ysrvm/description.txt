The name of this welfare scheme is YSR Vahana Mitra. It is also known as YSRVM. Launched on 1st June 2021, &amp;quot;YSR Vahana Mitra&amp;quot; is a Social Welfare Scheme by the Transport, Roads, and Buildings Department. In this scheme, the government provides financial assistance of â‚¹ 10,000/- per annum to Self-owned Auto/Taxi Drivers for expenditure towards insurance, fitness certificate, repairs, and other requirements. The applicant shall &amp;quot;own&amp;quot; and &amp;quot;drive&amp;quot; an Auto rickshaw / Taxi / Maxi Cab. The applicant shall possess a valid driving license to drive an Auto rickshaw / Light Motor Vehicle.  The objective of the scheme is to increase income and reduce the expenses of taxi repair by providing financial assistance to financially weak taxi drivers. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Andhra Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is Roads and Buildings.

It falls under the categories: Transport & Infrastructure, Social welfare & Empowerment.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant shall &quot;own&quot; and &quot;drive&quot; an Auto rickshaw / Taxi / Maxi Cab.
The scheme is applicable to the owners of passenger Auto rickshaws / Taxi / Maxi cabs. 
One Family is eligible to get benefits for one vehicle (auto or Taxi or Maxi cab) only. Family is defined as Husband, Wife, and minor children.
The vehicle shall be in the possession of the owner.
The bank account shall be in the name of the owner of the vehicle. The bank account of the beneficiary can be in any one of the scheduled commercial banks.
The applicant should not be a beneficiary under any GoAP scheme for the occupational groups.
The applicant/family members shall not be Government employees/pensioners. However, the families of sanitary workers are exempted.
The applicant/family members should not be income tax payees.
The monthly electricity consumption of the family dwelling unit (owned/rented) should be less than 300 units (calculated on the average of 6 months preceding the date of application)
The applicant shall not possess land exceeding 3 acres wet/ 10 acres dry/ 10 acres wet &amp; dry land.
In the Municipal areas, the applicant&#39;s family should not possess the property of more than 1000 sq. feet of residential/commercial builtup area.
The beneficiary should not be a GSTN holder.


NOTE 1: The ownership and license of different persons in the same white ration card are allowed. However, only one person is eligible for financial assistance in a family consisting of a husband, wife, and minor children on the same white ration card.
NOTE 2: If the beneficiary is Father/Mother/Daughter/brother and the driving license is in the name of the major son, the Father/Mother/Daughter/brother who is the registered owner of the vehicle is eligible to get the benefit, though both names are noted in different white ration cards.
NOTE 3: The beneficiaries who have driving licenses issued by other states shall apply for a change of address in the concerned RTO Office, concerned based on their place of residence which will be done expeditiously.
