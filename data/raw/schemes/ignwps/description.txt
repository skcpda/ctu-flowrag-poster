The name of this welfare scheme is Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme. It is also known as IGNWPS. Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme (IGNWPS) is implemented by Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India. It is a non-contributory pension scheme to provide social security to widows of poor family (BPL)  of the society. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. The implementing agency is State/UT Government. Additionally, the nodal ministry is Ministry Of Rural Development and the nodal department is Department of Rural Development.

It falls under the categories: Social welfare & Empowerment, Banking,Financial Services and Insurance, Women and Child.

Eligibility criteria include: The eligibility criteria under Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme (IGNWPS) is:The applicant must be a widow in the age group of 4079 years.
