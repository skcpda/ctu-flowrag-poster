The name of this welfare scheme is Distribution of Nuclear Poly Hedrosis Virus. It is also known as DONPV. The “Distribution of Nuclear Poly Hedrosis Virus” scheme by the Agriculture-Farmers Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu, provides subsidies for the distribution of Nuclear Poly Hedrosis Virus (NPV) to farmers raising seed farms with Pulses crops. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Agriculture Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Quantum of Assistance: 50% cost of input limited to ₹250/per hectare.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Farmer.
  The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
  The beneficiary should raise seed farms with Pulses crops. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Collect the application form from the nearest Agricultural Office.
Step 2: Fill out the application form with the required details and attach mandatory documents (selfattested, if required). 
Step 3: Submit the form to the concerned office:
&gt; Village Level: Submit the application to the Assistant Agricultural Officer.

&gt; Block Level: Submit to the Agricultural Officer or Deputy Agricultural Officer.

&gt; District Level: Submit to the Joint Director of Agriculture. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://www.tn.gov.in/scheme/data_view/7078 Tags associated with the scheme include: Agriculture, Farmer, NPV, Nuclear Poly Hedrosis Virus, Subsidy.
