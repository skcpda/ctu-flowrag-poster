The name of this welfare scheme is Award to Scheduled Castes Students Who Secured Top Ranks at State and District Level in Standard X & XII (Sahuji Maharaj Assistance). It is also known as ASCSWSTRSDLSXXII(SMA). The scheme “Award to Scheduled Castes Students Who Secured Top Ranks at State and District Level in Standard X &amp;amp; XII” is initiated by the Director Scheduled Caste Welfare, Department of Social Justice &amp;amp; Empowerment, Government of Gujarat. Under the scheme, the Scheduled Castes Category students who have Secured Top Ranks at the State and District Levels in Gujarat in Standard X &amp;amp; XII are given cash prizes. Cash prizes are awarded to the first three students who secure the highest ranks in both Standard X and Standard XII across all streams. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Gujarat. The implementing agency is District Deputy Director (SCW) Office And Director, Scheduled Caste Welfare Office, Gujarat. Additionally, the nodal department is Social Justice and Empowerment Department.

Eligibility criteria include: The students should belong to the Scheduled Castes Category of Gujarat.
   The students who have Secured Top Ranks at the State and District Levels in Gujarat in Standard X &amp;amp; Standard XII across all streams are eligible for cash prizes.
   There is no income limit. The application process is as follows: Under this scheme, the list of students is obtained from the Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Board and the award is given to the selected students accordingly.
