The name of this welfare scheme is Antayoshti Sahayata Yojana. It is also known as ASY. &quot;Antayoshti Sahayata Yojana&quot; is a scheme by the Department of Labour, Employment, Training &amp; Skill Development Govt of Jharkhand. This is a 100% State Sponsored Scheme. Only the residents/domicile of the state of Jharkhand can apply to this scheme. Applications for this scheme are accepted online through the official website of the department. The scheme provides â‚¹ 10,000/- for the last rites in the case of the death of the registered beneficiary. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Jharkhand. The implementing agency is Jharkhand Building And Other Construction Worker Welfare Board (JBOCWW Board). Additionally, the nodal department is Labour, Employment, Training and Skill Department.

It falls under the categories: Social welfare & Empowerment.

Eligibility criteria include: The deceased should have been a domicile in the state of Jharkhand.
The deceased should have been a worker registered with the &#39;Jharkhand Building and Other Construction Worker Welfare Board (JBOCWW Board)&#39;.
The deceased should have been between 1860 years of age.
