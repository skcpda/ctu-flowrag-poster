The name of this welfare scheme is Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare Department: Incentive/Award of Prizes-For Each Subjects. It is also known as ADTWIP. The scheme “Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare Department: Incentive/Award of Prizes-For Each Subjects” by the Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu, provides financial incentives to promote academic achievement among students from underprivileged communities. One Student each from Adi Dravidar/Tribal/Adi Dravider converted to Christianity for each subject is selected for the awards.
&lt;br&gt;

&lt;br&gt; The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: For plus 2 Examination: ₹2,000/.  For 10th Examination: ₹1,000/.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Student.
  The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
  The beneficiary should belong to the Adi Dravidar/Tribal/Adi Dravidar converted to Christianity community.
  The beneficiary must have passed the 10th or Plus 2 examination. The application process is as follows: Step 1: The interested applicant should collect the application form from the office of the District Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare Officer.
Step 2: In the application form, fill in all the mandatory fields, paste the passportsized photograph (signed across, if required), and attach copies of all the mandatory documents (selfattest, if required).
Step 3: Submit the duly filled and signed application form along with the documents to the Director of Adi Dravidar Welfare, ChennaiStep 4: Request a receipt or acknowledgment from the concerned authority to whom the application has been submitted. Ensure that the receipt contains essential details such as the date and time of submission, a unique identification number (if applicable).
