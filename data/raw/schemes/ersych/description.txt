The name of this welfare scheme is E-Rikshaw Sahayta Yojana. It is also known as ERSY. Chhattisgarh State Government started a scheme &quot;E-Rikshaw Sahayta Yojana&quot; for unorganized worker in 20 December 2016. The main objective of the scheme to empower the unorganized worker through provide them financial assistance. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Financial Assistance

Registered unorganized labor individuals will need to cover the expenses of ₹ 10,000 for scheme execution.
A grant of ₹ 30,000/will be provided by the Chhattisgarh Unorganized Worker State Social Security Board.
The remaining sum will be extended to the Rikshaw/Auto Driver as a bank loan, with the beneficiary responsible for repaying it within a specified timeframe and at the interest rate determined by the bank.
The Board will only transfer its contribution to the beneficiary&#39;s account once all the necessary loanrelated documents are submitted to the board.

Eligibility criteria include: Only auto/cycle rickshaw drivers who are registered with the Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers&#39; State Social Security Board are eligible for this scheme.
Registered workers must be between 18 and 50 years old.
Registered workers must be registered with the RTO.
Registered workers must have a commercial vehicle driver&#39;s license.
Workers must have been registered with the Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers&#39; State Social Security Board for at least 90 days.
Each beneficiary can only receive the benefits of this scheme once.
Beneficiaries who have already received benefits from any other equivalent scheme offered by the state government are not eligible for the benefits of this scheme. The application process is as follows: Unregistered applicant :
