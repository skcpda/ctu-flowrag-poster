The name of this welfare scheme is Sanction of Laptops - Andhra Pradesh. It is also known as SOLAP. The Department for the Welfare of Differently Abled &amp; Senior Citizens launched the &quot;Sanction of Laptops&quot; scheme to distribute free laptops to visually challenged, hearing impairments, speech impairments, and orthopedic challenges students pursuing professional courses. The A.P. Differently Abled &amp; Senior Citizens Assistance Corporation proposes to procure 200 laptops at ₹30,000/- each. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Andhra Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is Dept of Social Welfare, WCD.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Distribution of Laptops.

Category I: Parent/guardian income up to ₹15,000/per month Full cost covered.
Category II: Parent/guardian income from ₹15,000/to ₹20,000/per month Half of the cost covered.
Category III: Parent/guardian income above ₹20,000/per month Full cost to be paid by the beneficiary.

Eligibility criteria include: The student should be a native of Andhra Pradesh.
The student should be visually challenged, hearing challenged, speech impaired, and orthopedically challenged.
The student should be pursuing postgraduate or professional courses.
