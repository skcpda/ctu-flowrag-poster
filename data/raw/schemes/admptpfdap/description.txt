The name of this welfare scheme is Assistance For Delivery / Miscarriage Of Pregnancy / Termination Of Pregnancy To Female Differently Abled Person. It is also known as Admptpfdap. The Government of Tamil Nadu introduced &quot;Assistance for Delivery/ Miscarriage of Pregnancy/ Termination of Pregnancy to Female Differently Abled Person with a sum of Rs.6,000/- @ Rs.1,000/- per month for delivery and Rs.3,000 for miscarriage/termination of pregnancy is given to female differently abled person. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Welfare of Differently Abled Persons Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: A sum of Rs.6,000/@ Rs.1,000/per month for delivery and Rs.3,000 for miscarriage/termination of pregnancy is given to a female differently abled person.

Eligibility criteria include: Differently abled person holding identity card issued by the welfare board for the differently abled. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Visit the official website of the Chennai district.
Step 2: Click on the Departments. Select Differently Abled Welfare, after selecting click on Differently Abled Welfare Schemes. 
Step 3: You will land on the List of Scheme page of Differently Abled Welfare Schemes.
Step 4: Scroll down to the section &quot;L. Tamilnadu differently abled welfare board schemes&quot;. 
Step 5: Download the application form of the &quot;S.No. 6 Assistance for delivery/miscarriage of pregnancy/termination of pregnancy to female differently abled person&quot;.
