The name of this welfare scheme is Mukhyamantri Medhavi Vidhyarti Yojana. It is also known as MMVY. The Government of Madhya Pradesh initiated the Mukhyamantri Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojana. The objective of the scheme is to provide promising career opportunities to meritorious and talented students The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Madhya Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is Technical Education and Man Power Planning Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Under the scheme, admission fee and actual fee (excluding mess fee and caution money) which is approved by Fee Regulatory Committee or M.P. Expenditure at graduation level as fixed by Private University Regulatory Commission or Government of India/State Government as the fee, Only that payment will be made.

Eligibility criteria include: The student is a resident of Madhya Pradesh.
   The income of the father/guardian of the student is less than Rs.00 lakh. 
   The student has secured 70% or more marks in the 12th examination conducted by the Board of Secondary education or 85% or more marks in the 12th examination conducted by the CBSE/ICSE.
   Student&amp;#39;s Entrance Exam Information (Whichever is applicable) 


(i) Has secured a rank up to 1 lakh 50 thousand in JEE Mains examination and has taken admission in BE/B.Tech course.


(ii) Have taken National Eligibility and Entrance Test (NEET) for admission to MBBS/BDS course.


(iii) Have taken admission in law course through CLAT (Common Law Admission Test).
(iv) Have taken admission in the medical institutions conducting MBBS/BDS courses of the Central Government on the basis of other entrance examinations.
(v) has taken admission to a law course at the National Law Institute, New Delhi, or the University of Delhi, New Delhi on the basis of his/her own entrance examination.
