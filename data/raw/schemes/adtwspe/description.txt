The name of this welfare scheme is Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare Department: Scholarship-Public Examination Fee for 10th and 12th Std.. It is also known as ADTWSPE. The scheme “Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare Department: Scholarship-Public Examination Fee for 10th and 12th Std.” by the Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu, is designed to support students from the Adi Dravidar, Tribal, and Adi Dravidar Converted to Christianity communities. Under this scheme, the government reimburses the public examination fees for 10th and 12th-standard students directly to the Directorate of Government Examinations. There is no income limit for eligibility under this scheme. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Reimbursement: Full reimbursement on Public Examination fee for 10th and 12th Std. students.
&lt;br&gt;

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Student.
  The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
  The beneficiary should belong to the Adi Dravidar/Tribal/Adi Dravidar converted to Christianity community.
  The beneficiary must be appearing for public examinations of 10th/12th Std. The application process is as follows: Students/ Guardians should contact the Headmaster of the School with all the required documents as directed by the authority.
