The name of this welfare scheme is Mukhyamantri Asangathit Karmakar Cycle Sahayata Yojana. It is also known as MAKCSY. Labor Department of Chhattisgarh State started this scheme &quot;Mukhyamantri Asangathit Karmakaar Saayakal Sahaayata Yojana&quot; for unorganized worker on 28 April 2012. Under this scheme, One cycle will be provided free of cost to each women beneficiary. The main objective of the scheme to empower the unorganized female worker through provide them free cycle. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: > A free of cost cycle will be provided.

Eligibility criteria include: Applicant must be an unorganized female worker.
Applicant registered in any district of the Chhattisgarh state.
Applicant must be between the ages of 18 and Applicant must not be receiving benefits under any other parallel scheme of the State Government for the same purpose. The application process is as follows: Unregistered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Asangathit Shramik Panjikaran&quot;
   What do you want to do : &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Check the eligibility criteria for registration in Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board and click next. 
Fill out the online &quot;Registration form&quot;.
Submit.





Already Registered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Yojana&quot;
   What do you want to do : &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Select your district name and provide old/new registration number and click next.
Select the scheme name. 
Fill out the online with required details.
