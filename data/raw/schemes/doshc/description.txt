The name of this welfare scheme is Distribution of Soil Health Card. It is also known as DOSHC. Under the “Distribution of Soil Health Card” scheme, by the Agriculture-Farmers Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu, Soil health cards will be distributed to all the farmers who have offered the soil sample for testing by paying a prescribed fee of ₹5/- each for Macro and Micronutrient testing.
&lt;br&gt; The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Agriculture Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Soil Health Cards to farmers.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Farmer.
  The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
  The beneficiary should have provided soil samples for testing.
  The beneficiary should have paid the prescribed fee ₹5/each for Macro and Micronutrient testing. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Collect the application form from the nearest Agricultural Office.
Step 2: Fill out the application form with the required details and attach mandatory documents (selfattested, if required). 
Step 3: Submit the form to the concerned office:
&gt; Village Level: Submit the application to the Assistant Agricultural Officer.

&gt; Block Level: Submit to the Agricultural Officer or Deputy Agricultural Officer.

&gt; District Level: Submit to the Joint Director of Agriculture. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://www.tn.gov.in/scheme/data_view/6849 Tags associated with the scheme include: Agriculture, Farmer, Soil Health Card.
