The name of this welfare scheme is Khadi Karigar Janashree Bima Yojana. It is also known as KKJBY. A group insurance scheme by MoMSME for khadi artisans (spinners and weavers). The scheme provides insurance cover against normal death, accidental death, permanent or partial disability of the insured artisan. The artisan must be member of the approved vocation / occupation groups. This scheme was formulated by Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) in association with the Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC). The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. The implementing agency is Pension Group Schemes Department, Life Insurance Corporation Of India (LIC). Additionally, the nodal ministry is Ministry Of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises.

It falls under the categories: Banking,Financial Services and Insurance, Social welfare & Empowerment, Education & Learning, Business & Entrepreneurship.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant must be a Khadi Artisan (Khadi Karigar).
The age of the applicant must be between 18 and 59 years.
The applicant must be a member of the approved vocation/occupation groups.
The group must have a minimum of 25 members. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://my.msme.gov.in/MyMsmeMob/MsmeScheme/Pages/1_3_1.html Tags associated with the scheme include: Business, Citizen Empowerment, Entrepreneur, Insurance, Persons With Disability.
