The name of this welfare scheme is Gargi Puruskar Yojana. It is also known as GPY. Through the Gargi Puraskar Yojana, girls are given incentives from the government to move forward in the field of education. Girls of all classes can take advantage of the scheme. The amount of this scheme will be given every year on Basant Panchami.
&lt;br&gt;

The girl students who will get the benefit of the scheme in class 10th will be required to enrol in the school in classes 11th and 12th. And the government will give the amount to the beneficiary girl child through a check, so it is mandatory for the girl child to have a bank account. You can apply for the scheme online. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Rajasthan. Additionally, the nodal department is Education (Elementary and Secondary) Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Payable for 11 &amp; 12 class girl students: Rs. 3,000/each year on the day of Basant Panchami.

Eligibility criteria include: The student must have secured minimum 75% marks in class 10th.
The girl student should be studying regularly in class 11 or There is no restriction on income, occupation and caste etc. of the parents of the student. The application process is as follows: Scholarship Application online through School Principal on Shaladarpan Portal.Click on &quot;Balika Shiksha Protsahan&quot; or click here. Select the scheme and click on apply button. 
Now select the scheme name. 
Fill out the required details and process further. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://schemes.rajasthan.gov.in/scheme/detail/326 Tags associated with the scheme include: Financial Assistance, Girl Education, Scholarship.
