The name of this welfare scheme is Chief Minister’s Solar Rooftop Capital Incentive Scheme. It is also known as CMSRCIS. Chief Minister’s Solar Rooftop Capital Incentive Scheme is a capital subsidy scheme by the Tamil Nadu Government. In this scheme, a capital subsidy of ₹ 20,000 per kilowatt is provided for grid-connected domestic solar PV systems in addition to the 30% subsidy scheme of the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) of the Government of India. For individual homes/flats, the solar system capacity shall be 1 kW. For the residential flats solar system capacities of 5 kW, 10 kW, and multiples thereof can be applied for common usage as a group application.
( limited to 30% of MNRE benchmark cost or 30% of the project cost, whichever is less) The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Family, Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. The implementing agency is Tamil Nadu Energy Development Agency (TEDA). Additionally, the nodal department is Energy Department.

It falls under the categories: Utility & Sanitation.

Eligibility criteria include: Eligibility for Consumer
The scheme is open to applicants from individual homes/flats and residential flats, having an electricity connection.


Eligibility for Vendor / Installer
Qualified Solar Installers empanelled by the Tamil Nadu Energy Development Agency through a tendering system under this scheme (www.teda.in/pdf/CISINSTALLERS.pdf) For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://teda.in/programes/cms-solar-rooftop-capital-incentive-scheme/ FAQs: https://teda.in/faqs/ Consumer's Guide: https://teda.in/pdf/Solar%20net%20metering%20Consumer%20Guide.pdf Application Form (Single Applicant Of Individual House): http://www.teda.in/pdf/CIS_Individual.pdf Application Form (Combined Group Of Flat Owners): http://www.teda.in/pdf/CIS_Group.pdf Tags associated with the scheme include: Renewable Energy, Solar Rooftop, Utility.
