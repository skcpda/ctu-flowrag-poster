The name of this welfare scheme is Economic Help To Tribal Girls For Higher Education (Class 11 & 12). It is also known as EHFTG. Rajasthan state government started a scheme &quot;Economic Help to Tribal Girls for Higher Education (Class 11 &amp; 12)&quot;. The main objective is to provide benefits to the tribal girls for their higher education, who are studying in government schools by taking admission as regular students for higher education. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Rajasthan. Additionally, the nodal department is Tribal Area Development Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Rs. 3,500/One time and fully in July

Eligibility criteria include: Should be studying regularly in 11th and 12th in government schools.
The girl student should belong to the Scheduled Tribe category of Rajasthan.
The parent/guardian/guardian/husband of the student should not be an income tax payer.
The student must be a native of Rajasthan.
The student who is studying by living in the hostel run by the government. They will not be entitled to benefit under the said scheme.
Must have Vaidya Bhamashah card and Aadhaar card. The application process is as follows: Applicant to log in to RajSSO Portal using his/her SSOID.If SSOID is not available then the applicant to first register on RajSSO Portal to create SSOID and update SSO Profile at first login.After successful authentication/login and updation of the SSOID Profile and select the scheme name. 
Select his/her name, the bank account and fill in personal details.
Upload the required document.
Click the “SUBMIT” button. For more information, please refer to: Details: https://schemes.rajasthan.gov.in/scheme/detail/457 Registration: https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in/register Tags associated with the scheme include: Educational Grant, Financial Assistance, Higher Education, Student.
