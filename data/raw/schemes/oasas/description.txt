The name of this welfare scheme is Old Age Samman Allowance. It is also known as OASAS. The scheme &quot;Old Age Samman Allowance&quot; is a social security scheme implemented by the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of Haryana. The scheme provides a pension of ₹3,000/- per month to senior citizens (60 years or above) who are unable to sustain themselves with their own resources. The scheme targets the needy and poorer sections of society, such as agricultural labourers, rural artisans, SCs/BCs, and small/marginal farmers. The scheme is implemented through the Zila Parishads. The pension is disbursed through the Panchayati Raj Institutions. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Haryana. The implementing agency is Department Of Social Justice And Empowerment, Government Of Haryana. Additionally, the nodal department is Social Justice and Empowerment Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Pension of ₹3,000/per month.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be 60 years or above in age.
The applicant should be domiciled in and a resident of Haryana.
