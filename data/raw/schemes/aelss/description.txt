The name of this welfare scheme is Abhinandan Education Loan Subsidy Scheme. It is also known as AELSS. The objective of this scheme is to provide a loan subsidy of up to Rs 50,000 on education loans to students pursuing higher education from good institutions. The Abhinandan Education Loan Subsidy Scheme has been started with the Housing Scheme. Under this scheme, the Government of Assam will provide a one-time subsidy on loans to all students pursuing higher education on education loans. Under the Abhinandan Scheme all major banks, all commercial banks such as  Federal Bank and HDFC are included.


In this scheme, the state government will provide subsidy to the family of those students who has taken an education loan of more than Rs 1 lakh. Students getting an education on an education loan can subscribe to this scheme online or offline The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Assam. Additionally, the nodal department is Finance Department.

It falls under the categories: Education & Learning, Skills & Employment.

Eligibility criteria include: Eligibility Criteria

It is mandatory for the student to be a permanent resident of the state of Assam.
The student must have taken a loan from a commercial or rural bank, otherwise, he cannot avail of the benefit of the scheme.
The bank has been recognized by the Reserve Bank of India.
The benefit of this scheme can be availed only as a subsidy on education loans.
