The name of this welfare scheme is Mukhyamantri Samekit Chaur Vikas Yojana. It is also known as MMSCVY. The &quot;Mukhyamantri Samekit Chaur Vikas Yojana&quot; was launched by the Animal and Fisheries Resources Department, Government of Bihar. under Seven Nishchay-2, aims to develop undeveloped and unutilized private land in Bihar for fish-based integrated aquaculture. With a budget of â‚¹2972.79 lakh, the scheme targets the sustainable development of 600 hectares of Chaur land


Objective:

- Utilization of unutilized private land for fish farming.
- Increase in fish production and productivity.
- Groundwater level improvement, supporting the &quot;Jal-Jeevan-Hariyali&quot; campaign.
- Additional employment opportunities for farmers.
- Strengthening of the local economy. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual, Family, Business Entity. It is specifically implemented in Bihar. Additionally, the nodal department is Animal & Fisheries Resources Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: > Beneficiarybased subsidy:


50% for other categories.
70% for Most Backward Castes, Scheduled Castes, and Scheduled Tribes.
40% Entrepreneurbased subsidy.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be an individual, family, group beneficiary, or entrepreneur.
The applicant should be a private landowner or lessee in Chaurdominated districts. The application process is as follows: > Registration Process:

Step01: The applicant visits the official website and clicks the &quot;Applications are being invited for all the schemes of the financial year (202425), click here to apply&quot;. 
Step02: Select the &quot;Registration for Fisheries Schemes&quot;
Step03: Select the category: Individual and complete all basic details of the applicant for registration, including category, caste, applicant name, date of birth, address, bank details, etc.
Step04: Enter a valid 10digit mobile number and click to send OTP.
Step05: An OTP will be sent to the provided mobile number.
Step06: Enter the OTP; after verification of the OTP, you will receive a Registration Number and Password.
Step07: Click on the &quot;Login&quot; and enter the Registration Number and Password


> Application Form Filling Process:

Step01: The applicant visits the official website and clicks the &quot;Applications are being invited for all the schemes of the financial year (202425), click here to apply&quot;.
Step02: Click &quot;If your already register, login here&quot;. 
Step03: Select &quot;Application form&quot; under the &quot;scheme name&quot; option on the top menu.
Step04: Applicant need to provide the following details :


  Personal details.
  Permanent address.
  Bank account details.
  Land details.
  Financial availability of the scheme.
  Agriculture, horticulture and agroforestry components


Step05: Upload the required documents


  Passport size photographs.
  Aadhaar card.
  Land ownership certificate/revenue receipt or lease agreement, etc.


Step06: Click on the submit button and a receipt will be generated.
