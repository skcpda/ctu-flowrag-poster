The name of this welfare scheme is Old-Age Pension Scheme. It is also known as OPS. The Government of Himachal Pradesh, Department of Social Justice &amp; Empowerment has launched Old Age Pension Scheme with the aim to provide social security to destitute elderly people of the state. In this scheme, a financial assistance of ₹ 850/- per month to pensioners aged between 60 years to 69 years and ₹ 1500/- per month to pensioners aged 70 years and above is provided. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Himachal Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is Department of Social Justice and Empowerment.

The scheme provides the following benefits: ₹ 850/per month to pensioners aged between 60 years to 69 years.
₹ 1500/per month to pensioners aged 70 years and above.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicants must be a resident of Himachal Pradesh state.
Such elderly persons whose age is 60 years or more and who do not have proper means of care/upbringing and who have minor children and whose family annual income from all sources does not exceed ₹ 35,000/Or;
Such elderly persons whose annual family income from all sources does not exceed ₹ 35000/are eligible for the scheme. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Download the Social Security Pension Application Form.
Step 2: Fill in all the mandatory fields, paste the recent passportsized photograph (signed across), and attach copies of all the mandatory documents (selfattested).
Step 3: Submit the duly filled and signed application form along with the documents to the concerned authority at:

Office of Panchayat 


Tehsil Welfare Officer 


District Welfare Officer.
