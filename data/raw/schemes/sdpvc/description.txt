The name of this welfare scheme is Sanctioning of Daisy Players to Visually Challenged. It is also known as SDPVC. The Department for the Welfare of Differently Abled &amp; Senior Citizens launched the &quot;Sanctioning of Daisy Players to Visually Challenged&quot; scheme to distribute free daisy players to visually challenged students pursuing 9th Class to Degree as per the Action Plan 2017-18. The Assistant Directors and District Managers are instructed to follow these guidelines to ensure proper distribution. The application is accepted online for this scheme. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Andhra Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is Dept of Social Welfare, WCD.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Distribution of Daisy Players.

Category I: Parent/Guardian income up to ₹15,000/per month – full cost covered.
Category II: Parent/Guardian income from ₹15,001 to ₹20,000/per month – half of the cost covered.
Category III: Parent/Guardian income above ₹20,000/per month – full cost to be borne by the beneficiary.

Eligibility criteria include: The student&#39;s age should be between 16 to 23 years.
The student should be studying in 9th to degree courses. 
The student should be a native of Andhra Pradesh.
The student should be visually challenged.
