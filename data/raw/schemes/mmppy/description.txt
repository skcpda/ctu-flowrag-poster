The name of this welfare scheme is Mukhyamantri Prakhand Parivahan Yojana. It is also known as MMPPY. Under the Transport Department of the Bihar Government, has implemented the &amp;#39;Mukhyamantri Prakhand Parivahan Yojana&amp;#39; to provide transportation facilities connecting blocks and remote Panchayats with district headquarters. This scheme aims to enhance connectivity and facilitate mobility for residents across the state of Bihar. To encourage scheduled castes, scheduled tribes, extremely backward classes, backward classes, and minority communities entrepreneurship by providing financial support to purchase buses. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Bihar. Additionally, the nodal department is Transport Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Subsidy of ₹5,00,000/per bus to beneficiaries.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a resident of the block where they apply.
The age of the beneficiary should not be less than 18 years.
The beneficiary must have a driving license. 
The beneficiary should not be working/employed in government service.
The beneficiary should belong to the specified categories SC, ST, EBC, BC, Minority, and General. The application process is as follows: Step 01: The applicant need to visit the Transport Department’s official website for Mukhyamantri Block Transport Yojana registration.
Step 02: The applicant need to fill following details while registering : 


  Phone Number
  Password
  Repassword
  Email ID
  Driving Licence Number
