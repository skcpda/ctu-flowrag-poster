The name of this welfare scheme is Scheme for Supportive Money to the Parents of Transgender Children. It is also known as SFSMTTPOTC. The &quot;Scheme for Supportive Money to the Parents of Transgender Children&quot; was launched by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment. The scheme aims to support 5,000 beneficiaries annually, with the potential for higher numbers based on State/UT identification. Transgender persons face stigma and discrimination in almost every sphere of their life including within the group, health facilities, education, employment, etc. Awareness and information are needed among parents to support their gender-nonconforming or transgender children, setting aside their discomfort and deeply held normative attitudes. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Family. Additionally, the nodal ministry is Ministry Of Social Justice and Empowerment and the nodal department is Department of Social Justice & Empowerment.

The application process is as follows: Step 1: The parents/guardians should visit (during office hours) the State Government or UT Administration office. and request a hard copy of the prescribed format of the application form from the staff exclusively entrusted to issue and collect filledin applications.
Step 2: Submit the duly filled and signed application form along with the documents to the State Government or UT Administration office.
