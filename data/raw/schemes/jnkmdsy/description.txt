The name of this welfare scheme is Jharkhand Nirman Karmakar Mrityu/Durghatana Sahayata Yojana. It is also known as JNKMDSY. &quot;Jharkhand Nirman Karmakar Mrityu/Durghatana Sahayata Yojana&quot; is an Insurance Scheme by the Department of Labour, Employment, Training &amp; Skill Development Govt of Jharkhand. The scheme is also known as the &quot;Jharkhand Construction Worker Death/Accident Assistance Scheme&quot; in English. This is a 100% State Sponsored Scheme. Only the residents/domicile of the state of Jharkhand can apply to this scheme. The applications for this scheme are accepted online through the official website of the department (http://shramadhan.jharkhand.gov.in). The scheme provides social security to all registered workers in the age group of 18-60 years. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Jharkhand. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour, Employment, Training and Skill Department.

It falls under the categories: Social welfare & Empowerment.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a domicile in the state of Jharkhand.
The applicant should be a registered Construction Worker (Nirman Shramik / Nirman Karmakar).
The applicant should be in the age group of 1860 years.
The applicant should have worked for at least 90 days.
