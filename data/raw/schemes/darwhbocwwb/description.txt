The name of this welfare scheme is Disability Assistance For Registered Worker (HBOCWWB). It is also known as DARWHBOCWWB. The scheme “Disability Assistance for Registered Worker (HBOCWWB)” was launched by the Haryana Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board (HBOCWWB), Labour Department Haryana. Under the scheme, a lump sum financial assistance of ₹1.50 lakh to ₹3.00 lakh is given to registered workers on the basis of the percentage of disability which occurred due to an accident at the workplace during the course of employment. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Haryana. The implementing agency is Haryana Building And Other Construction Workers Welfare Board. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: On the basis of the percentage of disability, a lump sum financial assistance will be given to registered workers as under:
Sl. No. Percentage of DisabilityAmount  to be Paid 1    Upto 50% Disability     ₹1,50,000/2    51% to 75% Disability       ₹2,00,000/3     76% or above        ₹3,00,000/

Eligibility criteria include: The worker should be registered under Haryana Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board (HBOCWWB).
   The registered worker should have regular membership.
   The registered worker who has become disabled due to an accident at the workplace during the course of employment is eligible to apply under the scheme.

Note 01: It is mandatory to apply within one year of becoming disabled.
Note 02: The disability Certificates in regard to the percentage of disability issued by the Government Medical Board. The application process is as follows: Registration of a Construction Worker Under HBOCWWB:
&gt; Step 01: The construction worker should visit the Official Website of the Labour Department Haryana. 

&gt; Step 02: On the home page, click on the “Building &amp;amp; Ors Const. Workers Welfare Board” link.

&gt; Step 03: Applicant has to read all the instructions and then “Tick” the acknowledgment, then click on the “Submit” button.

&gt; Step 04: Verification using Parivar Pehchan Patra (PPP)Family ID:

&gt; a) Forget/not having Family ID: If you do not have PPP ID or forget the PPP ID, then you have to select that radio button and you are redirected to the PPP Portal for getting their PPP ID.

&gt; b) Having a Family ID: If you have a family ID, then only you can move to the next step for registration. To proceed further, follow the given steps:

&gt; • Select the “I have family ID” radio button

&gt; • Enter Parivar Pehchan Patra number Family ID

&gt; • Click on “Fetch Family Details” which displays the family members registered under the given Family ID

&gt; • Select a family member for registration

&gt; • Enter the OTP that is being sent to the selected family member for verification

&gt; • Click on “Click to Verify”

&gt; Step 05: Aadhaar Verification before Registration: Provide your Aadhaar number and tick mark the checkbox for the declaration. Click on the “Continue” button.

&gt; Step 06: After verification, fill out the complete registration form and submit the form.

&gt; Step 07: Login into the account: After the final submission of the registration form, a worker can login into his/her account using their username and password but unable to avail of any benefit. 

&gt; Step 08: To avail of the benefits, a worker has to pay the registration fee and add a work experience of a minimum of 90 days of the preceding year.

&gt; Step 09: Now, the worker needs to add work experience, to add 90 days of experience, the applicant has to fill in all the details where he/she has worked.

&gt; Step 10: The schemes and other benefits can be availed once the 90 days’ work experience is approved by the

&gt; Officer.

Apply for Scheme: Registration Process on AntyodayaSARAL Portal:
&gt; Step 01: To avail of the benefits of the scheme, the applicant needs to visit Official Portal AntyodayaSARAL Portal

&gt; Step 02: If the applicant is not registered on the portal, then he/she gets registered on the portal. 

&gt; Step 03: For registration, click on “New User/Register Here” and fill in all the mandatory details i.e. Name, Email ID, Mobile Number &amp;amp; password. Click on ‘Submit’.

&gt; Step 04: Applicant will receive the login ID on their mobile number.

Login to Apply for the Scheme:
&gt; Step 01: Visit the Official Portal AntyodayaSARAL Portal

&gt; Step 02: The “Sign in here” option is available on the right side of the screen. Applicant needs to fill in their credentials and click on the ‘Login’ button.

&gt; Step 03: In the opened window, click on “Scheme/Services list” and a list of schemes will appear on the screen.

&gt; Step 04: Now, select the scheme and click on “Apply for Service/Scheme”.

&gt; Step 05: Fill in all the details asked in the online application form and submit the application form.
