The name of this welfare scheme is Viswakarma Pension Scheme. It is also known as VPS. The Scheme “Viswakarma Pension” was launched by the Backward Classes Development Department, Government of Kerala to give a pension to traditional workers of Viswakarma Communities (Carpenters (wood, iron, stone), Goldsmiths, Moosari) who have completed 60 years of age in the Kerala State. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Kerala. The implementing agency is Backward Classes Development Department, Government Of Kerala. Additionally, the nodal department is Backward Classes Development Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: The monthly pension amount of ₹1100/will be given to the persons belonging to the traditional Viswakarma Communities.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a permanent resident of the Kerala State.
The applicant should belong to the traditional Viswakarma Community of Kerala State.
The age of the applicant should be more than 60 years.
The annual family income of the applicant should be ₹1,00,000/. The application process is as follows: Step 01: The applicant should visit the Official Website of the Backward Classes Development Department, Government of Kerala, and download the application form.
Step 02: Fill out the application form completely and send it along with all the relevant documents to the following addresses:
