The name of this welfare scheme is Safai Karmakar Hetu Aavashyak Upakaran Sahayata Yojana ​. It is also known as SKHAUSY. Labor Department of Chhattisgarh State started this scheme &amp;amp;quot;Safai Karmakar Hetu Aavashyak Upakaran Sahayata Yojana&amp;amp;quot; for unorganized worker on 02 September 2015. The main objective of the scheme to empower unorganized worker by providing required equipment for their work. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Registered sanitation workers will receive a grant of ₹ 700/(Indian Rupees seven hundred only) to purchase essential equipment such as gum boots, gloves, masks, and aprons.
Assistance amount will be provided every year.

Eligibility criteria include: Applicant must be registered with the State Social Security Board in any district of Chhattisgarh.
Applicant should not receive assistance from any other parallel scheme of the State Government.
Applicant should be between 18 and 60 years. The application process is as follows: Unregistered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Asangathit Shramik Panjikaran&quot;
   What do you want to do :  &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Check the eligibility criteria for registration in Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board and click next. 
Fill out the online &quot;Registration form&quot;.
Submit.





Already Registered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Yojana&quot;
   What do you want to do :  &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Select your district name and provide old/new registration number and click next.
Select the scheme name. 
Fill out the online with required details.
