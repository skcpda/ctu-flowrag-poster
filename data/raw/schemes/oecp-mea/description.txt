The name of this welfare scheme is OEC Pre-matric Educational Assistance. It is also known as OECP-MEA. The Scheme “OEC Pre-matric Educational Assistance” was started by the Backward Classes Development Department, Government of Kerala. In the state, the most backward communities from among the Other Backward Communities are grouped as Other Eligible Communities (OEC). The scheme is intended to provide educational assistance to pre-matric students belonging to these communities who are studying in Government/Aided/Unaided schools from Classes 1st to 10th. This scheme is being fully implemented with the state fund. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Kerala. The implementing agency is Govt./Aided/Recognised Un-Aided/C.B.S.E/I.C.S.E Affiliated Schools And Kendriya Vidyalayas In Kerala State. Additionally, the nodal department is Backward Classes Development Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Educational Assistance Benefits:
Sl. No.Classes/StandardsEducational Assistance Amount1I to IV ₹320/2V to VII₹630/3VIII to X ₹940/

Eligibility criteria include: The students should belong to the Other Eligible Communities (OEC) &amp;amp; Other Equivalent Communities of Kerala State.
The students should be studying in Government/Aided/Recognized UnAided/C.B.S.E/I.C.S.E Affiliated Schools and Kendriya Vidyalayas in Kerala state.
The students must be studying in Class 1st to 10th.
There is no annual family income limit for the students who belong to OEC Communities.
The annual family income should be ₹6,00,000/for the students who belong to Other Equivalent Communities. The application process is as follows: Step 01: To get the benefits of the scheme, the students do not have to submit separate applications.
Step 02: The School authorities should make an online entry through the scholarship portal within the prescribed time. 
Step 03: The concerned Educational Officer should do ontime verification of the details entered by the Unaided/ Affiliated Institutions.
Step 04: The school authorities will apply online for the scheme and financial assistance reaches the eligible students’ bank account through Direct Beneficiary Transfer (DBT).
