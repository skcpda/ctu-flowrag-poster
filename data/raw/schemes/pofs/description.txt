The name of this welfare scheme is Production of Foundation Seeds. It is also known as POFS. The scheme “Production of Foundation Seeds” by the Agriculture-Farmers Welfare Department, Tamil Nadu, is designed to support farmers in the production of foundation seeds, particularly for pulses. Eligible farmers receive a subsidy. Special benefits are provided for SC/ST farmers (24%) and farm women (20%). The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Tamil Nadu. Additionally, the nodal department is Agriculture Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Financial Assistance: ₹1,000/per quintal.

Eligibility criteria include: The beneficiary should be a Farmer.
The beneficiary should be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
The beneficiary should raise seed farms with Pulses crop. The application process is as follows: Step 1: Collect the application form from the nearest Agricultural Office.
Step 2: Fill out the application form with the required details and attach mandatory documents (selfattested, if required). 
Step 3: Submit the form to the concerned office:
&gt; Village Level: Submit the application to the Assistant Agricultural Officer.

&gt; Block Level: Submit to the Agricultural Officer or Deputy Agricultural Officer.

&gt; District Level: Submit to the Joint Director of Agriculture. For more information, please refer to: Guidelines: https://www.tn.gov.in/scheme/data_view/6891 Tags associated with the scheme include: Agriculture, Farmer, Foundation Seeds, Seed Production.
