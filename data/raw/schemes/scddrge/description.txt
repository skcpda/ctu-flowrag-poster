The name of this welfare scheme is Scholarship for Children of Deceased/Disabled/Retired Government Employees. It is also known as SCDDRGE. The scheme &amp;quot;Scholarship for Children of Deceased/Disabled/Retired Government Employees&amp;quot; was launched by the School Education Department, Government of Madhya Pradesh, to motivate children of government employees who are deceased, retired, or disabled. Under this scheme, scholarships ranging from ₹50/- to ₹250/- are provided to children of these government employees who are studying in Class 1 to Class 12 in government schools. To be eligible, students should not be beneficiaries of any other scholarship and must have passed their previous class. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Madhya Pradesh. The implementing agency is School Education Department, Government Of Madhya Pradesh. Additionally, the nodal department is School Education Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Under this scheme, scholarships will be given at the following rates:Class 1 to 5:   ₹50/per year for Boys and Girls studentsClass 6 to 8:   ₹120/per year for Boys and Girls studentsClass 9 to 12:  ₹250/per year for Boys and Girls students
Note: The scholarship will be deposited directly into the student&#39;s bank account.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant should be a permanent resident of Madhya Pradesh.
   The student should be studying in Class 1 to Class The student should be studying at a government school in Madhya Pradesh.
   The student should be the child of a deceased, disabled, or retired government employee.
   The student should not be a beneficiary of any other scholarship.
   The student must have passed the previous class. The application process is as follows: To avail of the benefits under this scheme, eligible students can contact the Principal of their respective government schools/institutions.
