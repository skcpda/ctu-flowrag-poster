The name of this welfare scheme is Pathari Kshetr Talab Nirman Aadharit Matsya Paalan ki Yojana. It is also known as PKTNAMPY. The &quot;Pathari Kshetr Talab Nirman Aadharit Matsya Paalan ki Yojana&quot; was launched by the Animal and Fisheries Resources Department, Government of Bihar. The scheme aims to promote fisheries by constructing ponds and setting up related supporting units in plateau-dominated districts. The initiative is designed to create new employment opportunities and provide a means of livelihood for farmers. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual, Family. It is specifically implemented in Bihar. Additionally, the nodal department is Animal & Fisheries Resources Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Maximum Subsidy per Beneficiary: 80% of â‚¹70 lakh per acre.Subsidy Installments: 60% after pond construction, 40% after installation of related units and purchase of inputs.

Eligibility criteria include: The applicant must own or lease land in the identified plateaudominated districts. 
A person/family should construct a pond of a maximum of one acre and a minimum 4acre water area i.e. 5acre area.
The applicant should install a concrete board on the pond. 
The board should be made of an iron sheet of size 4 feet x 3 feet, standing four feet at an iron angle, on which the description will be written with paint. The application process is as follows: > Registration Process:

Step01: The applicant visits the official website and clicks the &quot;Applications are being invited for all the schemes of the financial year (202425), click here to apply&quot;. 
Step02: Select the &quot;Registration for Fisheries Schemes&quot;
Step03: Select the category: Individual and complete all basic details of the applicant for registration, including category, caste, applicant name, date of birth, address, bank details, etc.
Step04: Enter a valid 10digit mobile number and click to send OTP.
Step05: An OTP will be sent to the provided mobile number.
Step06: Enter the OTP; after verification of the OTP, you will receive a Registration Number and Password.
Step07: Click on the &quot;Login&quot; and enter the Registration Number and Password.


> Application Form Filling Process:

Step01: The applicant visits the official website and clicks the &quot;Applications are being invited for all the schemes of the financial year (202425), click here to apply&quot;.
Step02: Click &quot;If your already register, login here&quot;. 
Step03: Select &quot;Scheme name&quot; under the &quot;Matsya Yojana&quot; option on the top menu.
Step04: Applicant need to provide the following details :


  Details of individual (applicant/group leader)
  Permanent address
  Bank account details
  Land/pond details


Step05: Upload the required documents.
Step06: Click on the submit button as well print the application form and submit it to the District Fisheries Office along with attachments. 


Note: Download the Affidavit form and Consent form also attached with the application.
