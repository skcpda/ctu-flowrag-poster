The name of this welfare scheme is Asangathit Karmakar Samachar-Patr Hawker Cycle Sahayata Yojana. It is also known as AKSPHSSY. Labor Department of Chhattisgarh State started this scheme &quot;Asangathit Karmakar Samachar-Patr Hawker Cycle Sahayata Yojana&quot; for unorganized worker on 10 September 2010. Under this scheme, One cycle will be provided free of cost to each unorganized  beneficiary. The main objective of the scheme to empower the unorganized Samaachaar-Patr Hokar worker through provide them free cycle. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Chhattisgarh. Additionally, the nodal department is Labour Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: > A free of cost cycle will be provided.

Eligibility criteria include: This scheme is for unorganized newspaper delivery workers (hawker) registered in any district of the state.
Newspaper delivery workers (hawker) are not eligible for this scheme if they receive benefits from a parallel scheme. The application process is as follows: Unregistered Applicant :

All the applicant have to visit the official website Chhattisgarh Labour Department. On the home page click on &quot;Apply&quot; under &quot;Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board&quot;.Enter the following details

   Select Group Name : &quot;Asangathit Marmakaar Mandal&quot;
   Select Service : &quot;Asangathit Shramik Panjikaran&quot;
   What do you want to do : &quot;Aavedan&quot;
   Click next.

Check the eligibility criteria for registration in Chhattisgarh Unorganized Workers State Social Security Board and click next. 
Fill out the online &quot;Registration form&quot;.
Submit.





Already Registered Applicant :
