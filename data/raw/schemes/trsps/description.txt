The name of this welfare scheme is Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme. It is also known as TRSPS. Detailed 
Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme is started by the Social Welfare Department (Government of Uttarakhand) for physically handicapped women/men Farmers. Under this scheme, the government provides financial assistance of Rs. 1200 per month for disabled women /men who are engaged in agriculture work or agriculture-related business in the state. 
This scheme is effective from the 1st April 2014 and implemented by the social welfare department in Uttarakhand. The name of the scheme Teelu Rauteli was a female warrior and folk hero who lived in Gurad Village (Pargana Chaundkot) Garhwal, Uttrakhand. During the 17th century, she fought in seven wars between the ages of fifteen and twenty. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Uttarakhand. Additionally, the nodal department is Agriculture Department.

It falls under the categories: Social welfare & Empowerment.
