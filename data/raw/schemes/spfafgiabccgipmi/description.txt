The name of this welfare scheme is Scheme to Provide Financial Assistance in the Form of Grant-In-Aid to Bhajani/ Choir/ Cultural Group/ Institutions for Purchase of Musical Instruments. It is also known as SPFAFGIABCCGIPMI. &quot;Scheme to Provide Financial Assistance in the Form of Grant-In-Aid to Bhajani/ Choir/ Cultural Group/ Institutions for Purchase of Musical Instruments&quot; implemented by the Directorate of Art and Culture, Government of Goa, provides financial assistance in the form of Grant-In-Aid to the Bhajani/ Choir/ Cultural Groups /Institutions for the purchase of Musical Instruments of their choice. 


> Objectives


1. To encourage and sustain the Bhajani Mandals, Choir Groups, and Cultural Troupes who contribute to a great extent to the preservation of cultural heritage with their cultural and musical performances.
1. To give financial support to organizations/institutions for the promotion and preservation of age-old culture.
1. To promote local talent and to promote local cultural troupes. The scheme targets beneficiaries such as University / Institution. It is specifically implemented in Goa. Additionally, the nodal department is Art, Culture and Museums Department.

The scheme provides the following benefits: Lumpsum grants / financial assistance to the maximum extent of â‚¹ 15,000/per Group/ Institution for the purchase of Musical Instruments only.

NOTE: The Government / Department may restrict the number of groups/institutions in each category according to the availability of the funds.

Eligibility criteria include: The Mandal/ Group/ Institution/ Troupe should consist of at least a minimum of 10 members.
The Mandal/ Group/ Institution/ Troupe should be conducting regular musical performances like bhajans, choir singing, folk performances etc.
The Mandal/ Group/ Institution/ Troupe should not already have received the benefits of this scheme.
