The name of this welfare scheme is Financial Assistance For Persons With Special Needs. It is also known as FAFPWSN. Details
Delhi Social Welfare Department is implementing a disability pension which is fusion of State as well Central Government contribution. The scheme is known as Subsistence Allowance/Financial Allowance to person with special need. The grant is Rs 2500/- per month.
These are the below mention disability consider under the scheme.

   1. Blindness 
   1. Low vision 
   1. Leprosy-cured person 
   1. Deaf 
   1. Locomotor Disability 
   1. Cerebral Palsy 
   1. Autism Spectrum Disorder 
   1. Intellectual Disability (Mental Retardation)
   1. Mental Illness 
   1. Dwarfism 
   1. Muscular Dystrophy 
   1. Acid Attack Victims

&lt;br&gt; The scheme targets beneficiaries such as Individual. It is specifically implemented in Delhi. Additionally, the nodal department is Social Welfare Department.

It falls under the categories: Health & Wellness.

Eligibility criteria include: Eligibility 

The age of Disabled persons should not be more than 60 years (0 to 60 years). 
Family income should not be more than Rs. 75,000/p.a. from all sources.
A resident of the National Capital Territory of Delhi for at least 5 years preceding the date of submission of application.
The disability of the applicant should not be less than 40% The Medical Board of Govt. The hospital should issue the disability certificate. 
Should have a &#39;singleoperated&#39; account in any Bank or Post Office for receiving the payment through an electronic clearing system. This provision may be relaxed in the case of minors, mentally challenged applicants or those who come under the purview of Legal Guardianship as per the rules of the National Trust.
